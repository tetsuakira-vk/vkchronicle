With guitarist Ao Sakurai anchoring the project since day one, vocalist Shuuji Ishii (who joined in 2000), and bassist Kenjirou Murai cementing the rhythm section from 1996 onward, Cali≠gari created a sound that rejected VK's typical melodic accessibility in favor of angular, confrontational arrangements that challenged listener expectations.

Their theatrical live shows, often incorporating multimedia elements and provocative imagery, became legendary among devoted followers.
