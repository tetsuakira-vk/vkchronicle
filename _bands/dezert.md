Dezert is a Japanese visual kei band known for their theatrical aesthetic and darkly atmospheric sound that blends gothic influences with modern rock sensibilities. The band has built a dedicated following within the underground visual kei scene through their dramatic stage presence and emotionally intense performances. With their striking visual presentation and commitment to the theatrical elements central to visual kei, Dezert represents the genre's continued evolution in the indie music landscape.
