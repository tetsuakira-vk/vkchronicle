# 12012

12012 is a Japanese Visual Kei band known for their dark, gothic aesthetic and theatrical performances that epitomize the visual kei movement. Active primarily in the 2000s, the band combined heavy instrumentation with dramatic visual presentation, contributing to the mid-2000s expansion of the VK scene. Their music and visual style helped establish the conventions of darker, more avant-garde visual kei that influenced many acts within the Japanese underground music community.
