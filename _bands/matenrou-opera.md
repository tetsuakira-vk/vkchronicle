Matenrou Opera emerged as a distinctive force within Japan's visual kei scene, carving out their own aesthetic and sonic identity through a blend of theatrical rock and sophisticated instrumentation. The band represents a significant chapter in visual kei's evolution, bringing together the genre's signature visual extravagance with carefully crafted musical arrangements. Their approach to the visual kei formula emphasized both grandiose presentation and substantive compositional depth, allowing them to attract listeners who appreciated both the theatrical elements and the underlying musicianship that defined their work.

The band's musical catalog showcases their versatility within the visual kei and J-rock spectrum, with tracks like "終わらぬ涙の海で" (In the Endless Sea of Tears) and "GLORIA" becoming notable highlights that demonstrated their ability to balance melodic sensibility with the darker, more intense atmospheres characteristic of the visual kei genre. Through their career, Matenrou Opera contributed to the visual kei scene's artistic credibility during an era when the genre was actively exploring new directions. Their legacy reflects the broader diversity of visual kei, proving that the genre could accommodate both visually stunning presentation and musically ambitious storytelling, resonating with audiences who sought depth beneath the theatrical surface.
