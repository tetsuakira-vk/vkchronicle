<p>A fresh supergroup has emerged from Japan's Visual Kei underground: **AIKOKU BARRACUDA** (哀哭バラクーダ), and they're already locked in for their first live performance on June 27, 2026.</p>
<p>The lineup reads like a who's-who of the VK circuit. Vocalist **Kanwa** brings experience from the melodic acts **allure idea** and **KAUXS**, anchoring the band's vocal identity. On guitar, **Junpei** (formerly **SEEEK**) joins forces with **Akira**, who previously played in both **ETERNAL BEAMS** and **Mi:m**—a dual-guitar setup that promises layered, atmospheric arrangements. Bassist **YUSUKE** is a touring veteran with credits spanning **KIRISAME**, **Inv.e.ga**, **LABORAT**, and **SIK**, bringing serious groove credentials. Completing the rhythm section is drummer **Ryunoske** from **RESERVA**, rounding out what could be a powerfully tight unit.</p>
<p>The combination of these musicians suggests AIKOKU BARRACUDA will occupy an interesting space within Visual Kei. With members drawn from both the heavier underground circuit (SIK, LABORAT) and the more atmospheric melodic side (allure idea, Mi:m), there's potential for a sound that bridges visceral intensity with emotional depth—exactly the kind of hybrid approach that's been driving the VK renaissance these past few years.</p>
<p>The band's first live is still several months away, giving them time to craft their debut material and build anticipation. For Western fans, this is a golden opportunity to catch a new project early before it potentially gains traction within the Japanese scene. Supergroups don't form every day in Visual Kei, and when they do, they often become significant players in the movement's evolution.</p>
<p>Keep an eye on their **[official Twitter account](https://x.com/aibara_official)** for further announcements, setlist details, and ticket information as the June 27 date approaches. This is one debut live worth marking on your calendar.</p>
