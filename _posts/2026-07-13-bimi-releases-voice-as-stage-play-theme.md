Bimi is stepping into the spotlight with a dual creative venture this July. The artist has announced a new digital single, "Voice," dropping on July 15th, 2026—and it's been tapped as the theme song for the stage adaptation of *gift*, a drama that captured audiences on Tokyo MX and BS Nippon Television last autumn.

![Bimi promotional image](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F01%2F19151738%2FBimi_%25E6%2596%25B0%25E3%2582%25A2%25E3%2583%25BC%25E3%2583%2586%25E3%2582%25A3%25E3%2582%25B9%25E3%2583%2588%25E3%2583%2593%25E3%2582%25B8%25E3%2583%25A5%25E3%2582%25A2%25E3%2583%25AB.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

"Voice" explores the emotional turbulence stirred by a loved one's words and presence—that unsettling echo that lingers after human connection. The song, penned and composed by Bimi with production from DJ dip, dives into themes of emotional fragility and the bittersweet nature of ambiguous love. It's a deeply introspective piece, layered with the kind of vulnerability that resonates long after the final note fades.

Beyond the music, Bimi takes on an acting role in the stage production itself, portraying Hayato Yuhara, a special crimes task force operative with dark ties to the conspiracy at *gift*'s heart. The stage play adapts the original drama's premise—a detective who reads minds through touch, a criminal who controls others with his voice, and a conspiracy involving illegal pharmaceuticals wrapped in cult ideology. The ensemble cast includes returning drama actors alongside fresh faces, all converging on a mystery that deepens across the narrative.

The stage version opens July 17th at the Shinagawa Prince Hotel Stellar Ball and runs through July 27th, 2026. Expect the theatrical intensity that drew viewers to the original series, now adapted for live performance.

![Stage key visual](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F07%2F13131926%2F1-27.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

Bimi's expanding into acting while maintaining musical momentum—a rare balancing act that showcases genuine creative range. The single releases via Evil Line Records on all major streaming platforms. You can pre-add "Voice" now through the official link, and catch details on the stage adaptation at the official *gift* website and social channels. Two Live Gallery shows are also scheduled for September at Club Asia in Shibuya for those wanting to catch Bimi's live presence.

Head to Bimi's official website and streaming platforms to experience "Voice" when it drops, and follow their social channels for the latest updates on both the music and theatrical projects ahead.
