<p>Japanese Visual Kei outfit ORSEAS is gearing up to drop fresh material this spring. The band has announced a new digital single titled "冥水ラカンス" (Meisui Rakans), set for release on April 25, 2026.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with ORSEAS, the band represents the contemporary wave of Visual Kei that continues to push theatrical boundaries while maintaining the genre's signature blend of heavy instrumentation and androgynous aesthetics. They're part of the ongoing renaissance of VK acts who've captured attention both domestically in Japan and internationally among Western fans hungry for the genre's distinctive visual and sonic approach.</p>
<p>While details remain sparse at this stage—typical for Japanese labels who often roll out information gradually—the artwork suggests the single will maintain the band's established aesthetic. The imagery hints at the darker, more introspective side of Visual Kei, with visual presentation that should appeal to fans of acts exploring moody, atmospheric territories.</p>
<p>Digital releases have become increasingly important for Visual Kei bands looking to reach global audiences. Streaming platforms provide immediate accessibility for Western fans who might otherwise struggle to source physical copies or navigate Japanese online retailers. This format also allows ORSEAS to test new material and maintain momentum between larger releases.</p>
<p>Mark your calendars for April 25, 2026, and keep an eye on the band's official channels for additional details as the release date approaches. If you're already following ORSEAS, this is essential listening. If you haven't checked them out yet, this could be your entry point into what they're currently creating.</p>
