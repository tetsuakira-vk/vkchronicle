<p>The third monthly installment of the New Releases Playlist for 2026 has landed, and it's packed with fresh material from some of the most exciting names in Visual Kei right now. Curated by the team at vk.gy, this Spotify-embedded collection features Luvielle as the cover artist and showcases the incredible diversity of the Visual Kei underground and beyond.</p>
<p>This month's playlist is a masterclass in why Visual Kei remains one of Japan's most vital rock subgenres. The 40+ artists featured span the entire spectrum—from established acts like SID, Acid Black Cherry, and Nightmare, who've been shaping the scene for nearly two decades, to hungry newer names like Kumori Nochi, Akai Yurei, and Mizukami CLINIC that represent the genre's future.</p>
<p>You'll find everything here: the theatrical intensity of XAA-XAA and Rorschach.inc, the gothic darkness of Dokubari and VALHALLA, the melodic hooks of Royz and The Benjamin, and the experimental edge of KAKUMAY and machine. Whether you're into symphonic elements, heavy distortion, or avant-garde arrangements, there's something to discover.</p>
<p>What makes this playlist particularly valuable for Western fans is that it captures a snapshot of what Japanese Visual Kei musicians are actually creating *right now*—not what streaming algorithms think we want to hear. The vk.gy team has always prioritized authentic curation, and it shows in the careful selection of both established favorites and emerging acts that deserve attention.</p>
<p>The cover and graphics were created by OmoideNoKage, adding an extra layer of visual polish that fits the aesthetic perfectly.</p>
<p>If you've been sleeping on any of these artists, now's the perfect time to plug in, press play, and let the theatrical chaos unfold. Stream the full playlist on Spotify, and follow vk.gy on Twitter (@vkgy_), YouTube, and Patreon for more curated recommendations and Visual Kei coverage. Don't forget to support these artists through CDJapan and other retailers when new releases drop.</p>
