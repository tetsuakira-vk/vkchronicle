Visual Kei's most enigmatic act is back with another installment of their monthly livestream series. CHAQLA., the band known for their striking visuals and deeply spiritual aesthetic, will broadcast the fourth episode of *CHAQLA.'s Room* on May 6, 2026 at 7 PM JST via the MAVERICK Channel.

For Western fans still discovering CHAQLA., the band has carved out a uniquely potent niche within the scene—their visual presentation is as meticulous and otherworldly as their sound is immersive and genre-defying. Each episode of *CHAQLA.'s Room* offers an intimate glimpse into the band's creative world through a series of member-led projects and guest collaborations.

This episode will feature **JiMYY from MAMA.** as the studio guest, joining mastermind kai for the fourth installment of his signature segment, **"kai Coffee Shop."** In this recurring feature, kai grinds coffee beans while hosting relaxed, natural conversation—expect both casual anecdotes and candid moments that only close friends could draw out of each other.

Bikky's cooking-focused project, **"Hibiki Kitchen,"** returns for its third outing, this time welcoming **Ryota from THE MADNA** as guest. The chemistry between host and visitor promises plenty of laughs and unseen banter that fans won't find anywhere else.

The livestream's second half transitions into **paid-member-only territory on Niconico Live**, where CHAQLA. and JiMYY will perform an **improvised jam session**—only the second time they've attempted this since creating the opening theme live during episode #1. Word is another "divine track" emerged from this session.
