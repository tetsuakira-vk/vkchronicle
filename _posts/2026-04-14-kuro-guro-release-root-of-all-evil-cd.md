<p>Japanese underground Visual Kei act **黒々 (Kuro Guro)** is gearing up to unleash their latest sonic assault with the release of *諸悪の根源 -The Root of All Evil-* on **April 26, 2026**.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with Kuro Guro, the band operates firmly within the darker, more experimental corners of the Visual Kei underground scene. Known for their uncompromising approach to both sound and image, they've carved out a devoted following among fans who crave authenticity and edge over mainstream accessibility.</p>
<p>The new album promises eight tracks of what the band does best. The tracklist reads as follows:</p>
<p>1. Intro</p>
<p>2. 序曲 (Overture)</p>
<p>3. URUSAI</p>
<p>4. Sleepless Nights</p>
<p>5. 空っぽ (Empty)</p>
<p>6. 諸悪の根源 -The Root of All Evil-</p>
<p>7. Instrumental Track Introduction</p>
<p>8. Menu & Extras</p>
<p>A crossfade preview is already available on YouTube, giving fans a tantalizing taste of what to expect. The preview showcases the band's signature blend of heavy instrumentation and theatrical sensibilities—classic Visual Kei done right, without compromise.</p>
<p>This release will be available at **M3-2026 Spring**, the massive independent music market event held in Tokyo, making it a must-hunt item for collectors and serious fans. For those unable to attend in person, keep an eye on Kuro Guro's social channels for further distribution details.</p>
<p>The album title itself—*The Root of All Evil*—suggests the band isn't shying away from darker lyrical territory, a thematic choice that aligns perfectly with the Visual Kei tradition of blending theatrical aesthetics with genuinely thought-provoking content.</p>
<p>If you've been craving fresh underground Visual Kei that refuses to play it safe, **mark April 26 on your calendar** and check out the crossfade preview on their YouTube channel. Follow **@kuro_guro_961 on Twitter/X** for updates on pre-orders and availability outside Japan. This is essential listening for VK fans who want the real deal.</p>
