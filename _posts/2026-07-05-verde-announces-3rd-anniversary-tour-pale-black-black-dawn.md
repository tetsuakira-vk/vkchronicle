Verde/ is marking three years since their inception with an ambitious nationwide tour that promises to be both a retrospective and a launchpad for what's next. The sonic project fronted by Shou of Alice Nine. has announced the "Verde/ 3rd Anniversary Tour 'Pale Black/BLACK DAWN'" — a five-stop journey culminating in a special finale at Tokyo's Spotify O-WEST on December 5th.

Since launching in December 2023, Verde/ has quietly built a distinctive identity that straddles rock with a crossmedia aesthetic spanning music, fashion, and visual storytelling. This tour represents a deliberate pause to reflect on that journey while simultaneously signalling a shift into uncharted territory. Rather than resting on past laurels, the band positions this anniversary cycle as a pivot point toward evolution.

![Verde/ promotional visual for 3rd Anniversary Tour](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2Fmain_low-1-580x725.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

The tour kicks off November 15th at Kawaguchi Hearts before moving through Kyoto, Nagoya, and Kawasaki. Each stop will feature a stripped-down version of Verde/'s current live set, building momentum toward the December 5th finale — dubbed "Final - 3rd Anniversary Live" — which will serve as the tour's centerpiece and most ambitious night.

For the main tour dates, Verde/ will field a powerful backing lineup including guitarist Yusuke and bassist Hikaru (both from Matenrō Opera) plus drummer KEN'ICHI. The December 5th special final adds keyboardist KAZAMI from DaizyStripper, elevating the arrangement into a full-band spectacular designed to encapsulate three years of musical and artistic development while pointing toward what comes next.

All-standing tickets are priced at ¥6,600 (excluding drinks). Presale access begins July 5th at 21:00 through Verde/'s official fan club on Fanicon, running through July 26th at 23:59. Additionally, a new single titled "Reimei/" drops July 12th across streaming platforms.

For complete details, ticket links, and tour updates, head directly to Verde/'s official channels on X, Instagram, and YouTube — that's where you'll find the latest announcements and behind-the-scenes content leading up to each show.
