GLAY's guitarist and songwriter HISASHI has stepped into the anime world in a major way, providing the opening theme song "Dead Or Alive" for the third season of *Record of Ragnarok* (*Shuumatsu no Valkyrie III*), one of the most explosive action anime franchises in recent memory.

The legendary J-rock band—who've been defining the Visual Kei sound since the '90s—are now part of a global phenomenon. *Record of Ragnarok III* recently dominated Netflix, landing in the top 10 of 48 countries and hitting number one in five of them following its December 2025 release. The anime adapts the massively popular manga series (18 million copies sold), which has won numerous accolades including "This Manga Is Amazing! 2019."

To celebrate the anime's broadcast launch, Club-Zy released an exclusive special dialogue between HISASHI and voice actor Daisuke Namikaze, who voices the eighth-round combatant Beelzebub (a god-side fighter). In their in-depth interview, both discuss how they approached their respective crafts—music composition versus voice acting—while bringing their shared passion to this high-stakes divine battle story. The full interview reveals what drew them to the project and their creative philosophies.

The anime itself chronicles humanity's final stand against the gods themselves. Across a series of one-on-one battles called Ragnarok, humanity's chosen champions face the divine in a fight for species survival. By the time Season 3 opens, the score is tied 3-3. This season features the seventh round: the grief-stricken King Hades seeking vengeance for his brother Poseidon versus China's First Emperor.

*Record of Ragnarok III* airs on TOKYO MX, BS11, and MBS, with Netflix streaming all episodes globally.

GLAY's opening theme "Dead Or Alive"—written by vocalist TAKURO and arranged by GLAY and composer Seiji Kameda—sets the perfect tone for this brutal, belief-driven conflict. If you're into anime that doesn't compromise on animation quality or emotional stakes, plus you want to support one of Japan's most influential rock bands in an unexpected creative venture, check out Season 3 now on Netflix or catch the broadcast airings.
