<p>Visual Kei band **Jigsaw** is making a statement with both sonic and visual reinvention. The group has unveiled a striking new aesthetic to accompany their latest single, **-反吐-** (Hantō / Retching), set for release on April 16th.</p>
<p>The two-track single includes the title track alongside "気狂います。" (Kichikuimasu / Going Insane), giving fans a fresh taste of Jigsaw's theatrical, darkly atmospheric sound. The band has already shared a music video for -反吐-, which you can watch on their official YouTube channel—a visceral visual complement to their uncompromising aesthetic.</p>
<p>**About Jigsaw**</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with the band, Jigsaw operates firmly within the darker corners of Visual Kei, crafting intricate arrangements that balance melodic sensibilities with raw emotional intensity. Their imagery and presentation emphasize psychological depth and visual storytelling, placing them among the genre's more conceptually driven acts.</p>
<p>**The Visual Evolution**</p>
<p>The promotional imagery accompanying this release shows a notable shift in the band's visual direction. The new photos reveal refined styling that maintains Jigsaw's signature dark elegance while suggesting a more cohesive aesthetic vision. These aren't cosmetic changes—they represent the band's ongoing artistic evolution and signal a potential shift in their creative direction.</p>
<p>**What's Next**</p>
<p>The April 16th release marks an important moment for Jigsaw's trajectory. For Western VK fans, this is essential listening; the single demonstrates the band's commitment to pushing their sound while maintaining the theatrical intensity that defines their work.</p>
<p>You can stay updated on Jigsaw's announcements via their official X account (formerly Twitter). Make sure to grab the single when it drops, and dive into the -反吐- music video to experience the full conceptual scope of what they're building.</p>
