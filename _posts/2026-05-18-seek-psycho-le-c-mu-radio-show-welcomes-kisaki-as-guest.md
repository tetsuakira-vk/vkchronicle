If you've been following the Visual Kei scene's most compelling personalities, you'll want to tune in: seek from Psycho le Cému is bringing back his beloved "Fish Dance Hour" radio program on Niconico Live, and this time KISAKI—the visionary guitarist and producer behind some of the genre's most influential acts—will be joining the conversation.

The 36th installment of "seekのFish Dance Hour" airs on Wednesday, June 17th at 7:00 PM JST. Alongside seek and regular co-host Hoshiko Seiichi (credited as "御意見番," or the Voice of Opinion), KISAKI will dive into a discussion examining and contemplating Visual Kei culture itself—a fitting pairing given KISAKI's decades of creative influence across the scene.

For those unfamiliar with the series, seek's radio program has become something of an institution within VK circles, hosting an impressive roster of musicians, producers, and industry figures. The guest list reads like a who's who of the genre: members of Alice Nine., THE MICRO HEAD 4N'S, Psycho le Cému itself, La'cryma Christi, and countless other cornerstone bands have appeared across the show's previous 35 hours.

KISAKI's appearance feels particularly significant given his production work and his role in shaping the sound and aesthetic of multiple Visual Kei acts throughout his career. This isn't just casual chat—these are musicians and industry veterans genuinely analyzing the state and soul of the genre.

The broadcast will initially air publicly, but will transition partway through to club Zy. channel membership-exclusive content (¥330 per month, with the first month free). Members gain access to artist original videos, special interviews, exclusive photos, and priority ticket booking for the "stylish wave" event series.

To catch this episode, head to the club Zy. website and follow their Niconico channel for the broadcast link and membership details when the time arrives.
