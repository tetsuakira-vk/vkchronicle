A long-awaited collision is finally happening. Kii-ga-shima (鬼ヶ島), the solo project of Doubt vocalist Kii, will share the stage with mitsu in a landmark two-man live dubbed "Oni-Mitsu-Toki" (鬼みつ時) this October.

For those unfamiliar with the key players: Doubt remains one of Japan's most compelling contemporary Visual Kei acts, known for their theatrical intensity and Kii's commanding vocal presence. This two-man marks a rare opportunity to experience the frontman's independent creative vision alongside mitsu, promising the kind of unexpected chemistry that defines the best Visual Kei crossover events.

The announcement emphasizes this as a fan-requested pairing, and for good reason—both artists operate in adjacent underground spaces, and their aesthetics should create fascinating contrast and collaboration potential. Whether you've been following Kii's solo ventures or discovering mitsu's work for the first time, this is the type of event that can reshape how you understand both performers.

**Event Details:**

**Date:** October 20, 2026 (Tuesday)  
**Venue:** EDGE Ikebukuro, Tokyo  
**Show Name:** Kii-ga-shima vs Mitsu "Oni-Mitsu-Toki"  
**Doors:** 6:00 PM / Start: 6:30 PM  
**Ticket Price:** ¥6,000 advance / ¥6,500 day-of (plus separate drink charge)  

**Ticket Information:**

Pre-order (A1+): June 17 (Wed) 12:00 – June 24 (Wed) 23:59  
Payment deadline: June 26 (Fri) 1:00 PM – June 29 (Mon) 9:00 PM  
General sale (B1+): July 4 (Sat) 10:00 AM onwards  
Maximum 4 tickets per person.

Tickets are available through the official e-plus link. Entry order follows pre-order, general, then day-of tickets.

For inquiries or additional information, contact the organizers directly through their official channels. Mark your calendars—this one won't come around twice.
