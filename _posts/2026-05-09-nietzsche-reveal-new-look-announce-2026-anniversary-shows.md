Kansai-based visual kei outfit **Nietzsche** are making waves this spring with a striking new visual reveal and two major live announcements that will cement their place in the region's thriving underground scene.

For those new to the band, Nietzsche have been building steady momentum across the Kansai region with their theatrical approach to visual kei. The group—featuring Vo. 毒 (doku), Gt. 偲 (shinobu), and Ba. 垂 (sui)—blend melodic sensibilities with darker aesthetic territory, drawing comparisons to the fusion of melody and drama that defined classic visual kei.

The band's first major announcement is **垂 (sui)'s birthday celebration**, titled "WHITE 'CAT'ASTROPHE," taking place **July 26, 2026** at Shinsaibashi SOMA. The lineup reads like a who's who of Kansai and underground VK talent: fellow locals RENAME and ギロチン will appear, while notable guests include **Közi** (known for his work with ZIZ, ZA-VAT, and the legendary MALICE MIZER), plus SPEECIES' **SADIE PINK GALAXY**. Tickets start at ¥4,000 presale.

More significantly, Nietzsche will mark their **2nd anniversary with a headline show** called "Reign -レイン-" on **September 22, 2026** at Shinsaibashi VARON. This solo performance carries real weight in the underground circuit—a chance to showcase how far the band has traveled in just two years. VIP and standard presale tiers are available from ¥4,000–¥6,000.

Between now and autumn, Nietzsche maintain a steady live schedule across Kansai venues including JUZA, CLAPPER, OSAKA MUSE, and beyond—perfect opportunities to catch them before their anniversary show.

If you're tracking the Kansai scene or simply looking for fresh visual kei with genuine personality, Nietzsche deserve your attention. Grab tickets to either event via e+ (links embedded on the official announcement), and follow their socials for updates.
