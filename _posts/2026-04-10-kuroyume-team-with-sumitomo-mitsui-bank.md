<p>In a surprising crossover between Visual Kei and corporate Japan, legendary darkwave act **Kuroyume** has partnered with Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation's digital banking service "Olive" for a limited-time collaboration campaign launching April 10, 2026.</p>
<p>The partnership celebrates Kuroyume's remarkable comeback — the band reunited after a decade hiatus and has since tackled a nationwide tour, major festival appearances, and new digital initiatives. Now in their 50s, vocalist Kiyoharu and bassist Hitoki continue pushing creative boundaries, making them the perfect ambassadors for Olive's mission to introduce digital-first banking to their generation.</p>
<p>**Campaign Details & Rewards**</p>
<p>Running through June 12, 2026, fans who open a new Olive account (or switch their existing account) and maintain a minimum balance of ¥20,000 will receive exclusive limited-edition collaboration t-shirts. Full details and eligibility requirements are available on the official campaign page.</p>
<p>But there's more: in mid-April, a comedy-heavy promo video drops featuring Kuroyume's two members alongside "Midosuke," Sumitomo Mitsui's quirky original mascot character. Expect surreal, laugh-out-loud banter that highlights Olive's features in refreshingly unconventional ways.</p>
<p>**What's Next for Kuroyume**</p>
<p>Beyond the banking tie-up, the band has plenty on their plate. They're preparing to drop a complete rework album (two discs) scheduled for spring 2026, and they've announced summer dates at Tokyo's TOYOTA ARENA TOKYO (July 17–19) followed by GARDEN THEATER (September 6) as part of their "THE PERFECT DAYS TO DIE" series.</p>
<p>For a band that once epitomized 90s Japanese darkness and excess, watching Kuroyume navigate modern digital finance with the same theatrical intensity they brought to their music is genuinely entertaining — and a fitting reminder that Visual Kei legends don't retire, they just reinvent.</p>
<p>**Check out the full campaign details** on the Sumitomo Mitsui Bank website, and keep an eye on Kuroyume's official channels for the upcoming comedy video.</p>
