<p>CODOMO DRAGON have released a new live DVD capturing their landmark final show from the "New Standard" one-man tour—a performance that doubled as a celebration of the band's impressive 15-year career.</p>
<p>Recorded on November 14, 2025, at Spotify O-EAST in Tokyo, this release documents a pivotal moment for the theatrical Japanese rock outfit. The band's energetic setlist spans 19 tracks, blending fan favorites like "BONEHEAD," "Twilight Discord," and the crushing "HEMLOCK" with deeper cuts and encore performances that showcase their dynamic range as live performers.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with CODOMO DRAGON, the band has been a fixture in Japan's underground rock scene since their formation, building a loyal following through their visceral live performances and sonically adventurous approach to Visual Kei. Their music combines heavy guitar work, atmospheric synths, and emotive vocal delivery—hallmarks of the genre's contemporary underground movement.</p>
<p>The DVD is priced at ¥6,600 and became available on April 1, 2026. The release is limited to jersey edition copies, making it a collectible for dedicated fans. The full tracklist includes standouts like "I'm crying alone on the battlefield," "Distortion," "Bad!!," and the haunting closer "Thorn," ensuring this capture delivers both energy and intimacy across the band's catalog.</p>
<p>This live DVD represents more than a simple documentation—it's a time capsule of CODOMO DRAGON at a turning point, their 15-year milestone marking either an evolution or ending. Either way, the "New Standard" tour final feels like essential viewing for Visual Kei enthusiasts seeking authentic, underground Japanese rock that refuses to compromise.</p>
<p>You can preview footage from the show on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rTfcigCdZd8). If you've followed CODOMO DRAGON's journey or are curious about contemporary Visual Kei's heavier side, this DVD deserves a spot in your collection.</p>
