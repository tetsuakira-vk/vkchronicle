Jin-Machine has officially welcomed two longtime support musicians into the fold, marking a significant milestone for the Tokyo-based Visual Kei outfit. On May 9, 2026, during their one-man live "Koko Kara Ribbon" at Nishi-Eifuku JAM, the band announced that guitarists SHIGE. and bassist ふみ (Fumi) would transition from supporting roles to full-time members, solidifying a new chapter for the group.

The newly minted members have also received stage names befitting Jin-Machine's theatrical aesthetic. SHIGE. is now credited as **Vérfaire Silvaine Shige** (ヴェルファイア・シルベーヌ・シゲ), while Fumi has become **Alphard Alfört Fumiya** (アルファード・アルフォート・フミヤ). Alongside frontman featuring16, drummer Rubella Kimura Kaere, the refreshed Jin-Machine lineup is primed to push forward with renewed energy.

Known for their maximalist approach to Visual Kei—blending theatrical presentation with unpredictable song selections and a reputation for humorous stage presence—Jin-Machine has been steadily building a cult following in the Western VK community. Their decision to formalize the support musicians speaks to both the chemistry they've developed and the band's commitment to long-term creative stability.

The band isn't wasting time celebrating the announcement. Jin-Machine has already pencilled in a **two-day one-man live** at Shibuya's Club Malcolm for September 9–10, 2026. The shows promise deep cuts alongside fan favorites, with setlists curated specifically for the one-man format rather than typical festival bookings.

**Ticket Details:**
- **Venue:** Club Malcolm, Shibuya
- **Dates:** September 9–10, 2026
- **Time:** OPEN 18:30 / START 19:00
- **Price:** ¥5,000 presale / ¥5,500 day-of (plus one drink)

Presale applications open **May 9 at 21:00 JST** and close May 24 (lottery draw May 26). General sale begins July 11, 2026 at 11:00 JST via the Jin-Machine official website.

If you've been sleeping on Jin-Machine, the new lineup announcement is your cue to jump in. Head to **jin-machine.net** to secure your spot for what promises to be a chaotic, energetic celebration of this band's distinctive brand of Visual Kei chaos.
