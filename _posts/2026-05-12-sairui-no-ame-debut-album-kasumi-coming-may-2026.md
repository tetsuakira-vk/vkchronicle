Sairui no Ame is gearing up to make their mark on the Visual Kei landscape with their highly anticipated debut full-length, *KASUMI*, arriving on May 23, 2026.

For those unfamiliar with the four-piece, Sairui no Ame—whose name translates to "tearful rain"—combines atmospheric melodies with the dramatic sensibilities that define contemporary Visual Kei. The band features sena. on vocals and guitar, D-2-/Shun on additional guitar, めくる (Mekuru) on bass, and amika on drums. Their sonic palette balances ethereal production with heavier, emotionally charged arrangements that should resonate with fans who appreciate both the introspective and explosive sides of the genre.

While details on the full tracklist and specific production team remain under wraps, the band has already showcased their creative direction through singles like "愛惜" (Aishaku), which demonstrates their knack for crafting layered compositions that build atmosphere before dropping into heavier segments. It's the kind of dynamic songwriting that separates memorable debuts from forgettable ones.

The May 2026 release date gives fans nearly a year to anticipate what could be a defining album in their discography. For a band at this stage, a polished, focused first album is crucial—and Sairui no Ame appears to be taking that responsibility seriously.
