Visual Kei fans have reason to celebrate—山内総一郎 is stepping into the spotlight with his highly anticipated solo debut. Best known for his work in the scene, the artist is launching a bold new chapter marked by the release of his first album under the "A-UN" project, a venture designed to explore both his unchanging artistic essence and his evolving musical direction.

The album, simply titled *A* (あ), arrives on July 22, 2026, and serves as a symbolic marker of 山内総一郎's transition into solo work. Today, the lead single "Beautiful Notes" began its pre-release digital distribution, offering early listeners a taste of what's to come. The track exemplifies the album's diverse sonic palette—a collection of 11 songs that range from introspective pieces to upbeat, multifaceted compositions.

![Album artwork](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F07%2F10194428%2F1-24-683x1024.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

The tracklist balances previously released content with fresh material. Fans will recognize "GOOD FELLOWS," featured in a Suntory non-alcoholic beverage campaign, and "人間だし" (Ningen Dashi), which serves as the opening theme for TV Tokyo's drama series *晩酌の流儀４ ～秋冬編～*. Alongside these are compelling new offerings like "ソラネコ," "ぶらんにゅーでい," "スロウダンス," and the ethereal closing track "彗星たちのカンタータ."

**Physical Formats & Special Editions**

The limited first pressing (¥33,000 / ~$225 USD) includes a Blu-ray with three live recordings from his solo performances—*The Hole In The Wall*, *OCTOBER SONGS*, and *ぶらんにゅーでい*—plus an exclusive nylon track jacket. A standard DVD edition (¥3,960 / ~$27 USD) features music videos for "Beautiful Notes," "人間だし," and "旅にでる." The regular CD-only version costs ¥3,300 (~$22 USD).

**Tour Announcements**

To celebrate the release, 山内総一郎 is launching a nationwide tour across eight cities, beginning August 2 in Osaka and continuing through late September. Dates include stops in Yokohama, Nagoya, Sapporo, Fukuoka, Hiroshima, Sendai, and Tokyo.
