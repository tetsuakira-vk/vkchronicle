<p>OPHELIA are making waves with their latest visual update and second single release. The band has dropped *Souen Ga Nokosu Kokuin* (蒼炎が残す刻印, "The Imprint Left by Azure Flames") across digital platforms including iTunes, accompanied by a striking new music video that's now live on YouTube.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with OPHELIA, the band represents the contemporary Visual Kei scene with theatrical aesthetics and dramatic soundscapes. This latest single showcases their evolution as artists, with the accompanying visuals delivering the kind of intricate, cinematic presentation that defines modern VK production.</p>
<p>The MV, which dropped on March 14, 2026, features the kind of atmospheric imagery and meticulous attention to detail fans have come to expect from emerging VK acts. The band's refreshed visual aesthetic accompanying this release signals they're not resting on their laurels—this is a band actively refining their image and sonic identity.</p>
<p>**Live Announcement**</p>
<p>Beyond the studio release, OPHELIA has exciting news for Japanese fans: they're hosting their first solo live (*Gokuenka*) this summer at Osaka Shinsaibashi CLAPPER. This marks a significant milestone for the band, solidifying their position in the VK live circuit. For Western fans, this is a reminder of how vital the Japanese live scene remains for Visual Kei acts, even as digital platforms expand their reach globally.</p>
<p>**Where to Get It**</p>
<p>*Souen Ga Nokosu Kokuin* is available now on iTunes and major retailers including Amazon, CDJapan, and RarezHut, making it accessible to international fans. The music video is streaming on YouTube for those wanting the full visual experience.</p>
<p>If you're tracking emerging VK acts pushing the genre forward, OPHELIA deserve your attention. Check out the new single and MV—and if you're planning a Japan trip this summer, keep an eye on details for that Osaka show.</p>
