<p>After two decades, KALEN is coming back—at least for one night.</p>
<p>The Visual Kei band has announced a special one-day revival event to mark 20 years since their disbanding. The reunion will take place on **January 10th, 2027, at Osaka Muse**, and the news came directly from vocalist Nao, who has been reminiscing about his time in the band on Twitter.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with KALEN, the band was a notable fixture in the Japanese Visual Kei scene, known for their theatrical approach to the genre. While details about their original run are limited in the Western fanbase, Nao's recent social media activity has sparked renewed interest and nostalgia among longtime followers.</p>
<p>The reunion will feature **four of the original members**, though guitarist Erize unfortunately won't be able to participate in the event. Even with one member absent, this marks a significant moment for fans who've been waiting to see the band perform again.</p>
<p>**What's particularly interesting** is that Nao is actively engaging with the fanbase ahead of the show. He's using his Twitter to crowdsource ideas for merchandise, asking followers what kind of goods they'd be interested in seeing at the event. If you have suggestions—whether that's classic band tees, vinyl reissues, or something more creative—this is your chance to have a direct say in what gets produced.</p>
<p>This kind of fan interaction is refreshing and shows genuine interest in making the reunion meaningful for longtime supporters. It also hints that organizers are planning to make this more than just a quick nostalgia trip; they're building excitement and creating new ways for fans to connect with the band.</p>
<p>**Mark your calendars** if you're planning to be in Osaka in January 2027, and **follow Nao on Twitter** if you want to stay updated on ticket information, setlist details, and those merchandise options. This is the kind of reunion show that could set the tone for potential future activities—so showing up and supporting KALEN could mean more surprises down the line.</p>
