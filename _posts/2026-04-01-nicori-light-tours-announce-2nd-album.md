<p>Ex-Janne Da Arc members are ready to shift into a bold new era. Nicori Light Tours, the project featuring guitarist you and keyboardist kiyo from the legendary visual kei outfit Janne Da Arc, will release their second album "RED:SHIFT" on June 3, 2026.</p>
<p>The band, completed by vocalist ayumu, has carved out an intriguing space in the modern visual kei landscape by merging the technical sophistication and orchestral sensibilities both you and kiyo brought to Janne Da Arc with a fresher, more experimental approach. While Janne Da Arc dominated the 2000s with their brand of symphonic visual kei, Nicori Light Tours operates with a leaner aesthetic and more adventurous songwriting—a natural evolution for veterans unafraid to push boundaries.</p>
<p>"RED:SHIFT" marks a significant milestone for the project, following their debut. The album spans 12 tracks on the physical CD release, opening with the ominous "spectre" and progressing through a diverse palette that includes both English and Japanese-language compositions. Standout cuts like "runway," "INSIDE," and the closer "あなたと出逢うために生まれてきた" (I Was Born to Meet You) showcase the trio's gift for balancing haunting atmospheres with dynamic instrumental passages.</p>
<p>Notably, the digital streaming release differs slightly from the CD version—dropping "Silent Enemy" from the tracklist—so collectors may want to grab the physical edition for the complete experience.</p>
<p>The 12-track album demonstrates why you and kiyo remain vital voices in visual kei's ongoing renaissance. Their pedigree is undeniable, but Nicori Light Tours doesn't trade on nostalgia alone; instead, they're creating something genuinely fresh while honoring the theatrical, keyboard-driven traditions that made Janne Da Arc essential listening.</p>
<p>Mark your calendars for June 3, 2026. If you've been curious about what these veterans are crafting next, "RED:SHIFT" is unmissable.</p>
<p>Follow Nicori Light Tours on [Twitter](https://x.com/N__L__Tours) for updates and pre-order information.</p>
