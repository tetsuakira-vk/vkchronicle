Deshabillz are riding a wave of momentum following the swift sellout of their debut 8cm single "Mariana Snailfish" and its second pressing, released April 1st. The band shows no signs of slowing down, with a nationwide tour underway and a star-studded birthday celebration in the pipeline.

For those unfamiliar with Deshabillz, the outfit has been carving out a distinctive presence in the Visual Kei underground with their avant-garde aesthetic and dynamic live performances. The success of "Mariana Snailfish" signals growing support for the group's artistic vision among both Japanese and international fans.

**Upcoming Live Schedule**

The "Jiba, Heading North… Mariana Snailfish" one-man tour kicks off this weekend with back-to-back shows in Sapporo on May 9th and 10th at Crazy Monkey (tickets via L-Code: 12640). The tour continues with stops in Sendai (June 6th, Birdland) and Niigata (June 7th, Riverst), before culminating at EDGE Ikebukuro on August 22nd. Tickets are available through the venues' official ticketing platforms.

**REI's Half-Century Celebration**

Perhaps the most intriguing announcement is the "REI (Deshabillz) Half a Century Celebration" on July 7th at Ikebukuro's BlackHole. This isn't a typical birthday show—it's a collaborative event featuring REI (Deshabillz's drummer) performing with an impressive lineup of guest bands, including Chelsea, Kuro Nje, Hinan Gougou, Koki Yoki Jidai no Keishou, and a special appearance from 博士 (Markyuro). Advanced tickets are ¥3,500 (plus one drink charge), while day-of admission is ¥4,000.

With their debut single already sold out and a packed summer calendar ahead, Deshabillz are clearly poised for bigger things. Whether you've been following them from day one or discovering them now, their upcoming shows offer the perfect opportunity to experience their world firsthand. Grab your tickets while they last—these releases and events are moving fast.
