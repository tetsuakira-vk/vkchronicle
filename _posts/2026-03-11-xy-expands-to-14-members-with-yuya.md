<p>YOSHIKI's supergroup XY just made a major power move. The 14-member ensemble—now featuring NEWS vocalist Yuya Tegoshi in their ranks—has surprise-released a revamped version of their debut single "Crazy Love (XIV Version)" as of January 27, 2025.</p>
<p>For those new to XY: the group was born from *YOSHIKI SUPERSTAR PROJECT X Season 2* on Nippon TV, assembling a rotating lineup of Japan's most talented vocalists and musicians under the compositional vision of X Japan's YOSHIKI. The original "Crazy Love" landed as their debut single and quickly resonated with listeners for its passionate melody and raw emotional depth.</p>
<p>The expansion to 14 members was officially announced last year during the broadcast, with the full lineup reveal happening during Nippon TV's *This Year's Most-Played Songs: Annual Music Awards 2024*. That's when XY performed "Crazy Love (XIV Version)" for the first time—and social media immediately erupted. The performance trended hard, signaling that fans were ready for this new chapter.</p>
<p>What makes this iteration special is how seamlessly Tegoshi's vibrant vocals blend alongside JAY's delicate delivery. Rather than drowning out the original's charm, the expanded arrangement adds genuine depth and dynamism. The track retains everything that made the debut single brilliant while breathing entirely new life into YOSHIKI's sonic vision. Fourteen voices united, fourteen perspectives on love's contradictions and beauty.</p>
<p>This is quintessential YOSHIKI energy: ambitious, collaborative, and unafraid to reimagine success. Whether you've been following XY since day one or you're just discovering them now, "Crazy Love (XIV Version)" is worth your immediate attention.</p>
