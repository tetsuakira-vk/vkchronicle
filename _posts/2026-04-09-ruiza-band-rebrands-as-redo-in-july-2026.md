<p>A fresh start is coming for the Visual Kei scene: **Ruiza BAND will officially rebrand as "Redo"** effective July 3rd, 2026. The name change marks an exciting new chapter for this talented collective, which features an impressive lineup of musicians with deep roots in the Japanese underground rock scene.</p>
<p>The newly christened **Redo** brings together four seasoned performers, each bringing their own pedigree to the project. Fronting the band is vocalist **Seth**, whose resume reads like a who's who of notable VK and experimental acts—he's previously worked with AFTER IMAGE, AMADEUS, Art Cube, SHAPE SHIFTER, and Moi dix Mois, lending him considerable experience in theatrical and avant-garde performance. On guitar is **Ruiza** (ex-Distray, D), the namesake of the original band, whose instrumental prowess has been key to the group's sound. Anchoring the low end is bassist **Tsunehito** (ex-Rélude, SCISSOR, D), and powerhouse drummer **美景 (Mikage)** (ex-VII-Sense, chariots) completes the lineup.</p>
<p>For Western fans tracking the Visual Kei underground, this rebranding is worth your attention. The members' extensive background across various subgenres—from the symphonic ambitions of some past projects to the experimental edge of others—suggests Redo will likely blend theatrical aesthetics with sophisticated musicianship. Whether this represents a sonic shift or simply a symbolic fresh start remains to be seen, but the pedigree on display here is undeniable.</p>
<p>Band name changes are always significant moments in Visual Kei, often signaling lineup shifts, artistic reinvention, or simply a clearer vision for what comes next. Keep your eyes on Redo as July 3rd approaches—this could be the beginning of something special in the VK underground.</p>
<p>**Want to stay updated on Redo's debut material and live shows?** Check official VK community forums and social media channels for more details as the rebranding date approaches. This is one to watch.</p>
