<p>Following the triumphant conclusion of their *Maniac Heaven Tour Vol. 17*, THE BACK HORN is ready to ignite summer 2026 with a brand-new one-man tour that promises raw intensity and cathartic release.</p>
<p>**Who are THE BACK HORN?**</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar, THE BACK HORN are a legendary Visual Kei institution—a band that has spent decades crafting a signature sound that balances theatrical darkness with genuine emotional depth. They're known for their evocative imagery, intricate compositions, and uncompromising live performances that leave audiences spiritually transformed.</p>
<p>**What's coming in summer 2026**</p>
<p>The band just announced the *"KYO-MEI One-Man Tour" ~ Explosive Sound, Scorching Heat, Pure Land ~*, a six-show tour across five cities running from July through September. This marks their first self-promoted tour to include Gifu and Kobe in years—a significant moment for longtime fans in these regions.</p>
<p>The tour's concept—"Explosive Sound, Scorching Heat, Pure Land"—reflects a deliberate artistic shift. After the introspective, densely atmospheric *Maniac Heaven* series, THE BACK HORN are pivoting toward raw catharsis: thunderous riffs, unrestrained passion, and a kind of spiritual salvation achieved through sweat, noise, and shared body heat on a packed venue floor. It's essentially their vision of transcendence through rock and roll.</p>
<p>**Tour dates & ticket info**</p>
<p>- **July 25** – Gifu, Club Roots</p>
<p>- **August 1** – Kobe, Harbor Studio</p>
<p>- **August 8** – Kyoto, Kyoto Muse</p>
<p>- **August 23** – Tokyo, Shibuya Club Quattro</p>
<p>- **August 29** – Nagoya, Bottom Line</p>
<p>- **September 4** – Tokyo, Ebisu Liquid Room</p>
<p>Standard tickets are ¥6,000, with a special U-18 cashback offer (¥2,000 back with valid ID at the venue). Fan club "Ginga Yubokumin" members get early access until April 12; general sale begins June 20.</p>
<p>**Don't miss this.** THE BACK HORN in an intimate venue setting during peak summer is a spiritual experience, not just a concert. Grab your tickets and prepare to be baptized in noise.</p>
