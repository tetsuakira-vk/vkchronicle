<p>LAFFLESIA are back with their sophomore digital single, and it's arriving sooner than you might expect. The band has announced *étoile ~La fin d'un amour~* (Star ~The End of a Love~) for release on April 18th, 2026, continuing to build momentum following their debut.</p>
<p>For those new to LAFFLESIA, the band represents the contemporary wave of Visual Kei acts emerging in the mid-2020s, bringing theatrical aesthetics and emotional intensity to the modern VK landscape. While details about the band's lineup and musical direction remain relatively fresh territory for Western fans discovering them, the single artwork and preview materials suggest a continuation of their dramatic, melancholic approach.</p>
<p>The release will arrive on digital platforms, though whether it will be available for streaming or purchase—as their first single was download-only—remains to be confirmed. A preview video has already dropped via their official Twitter (@MAYU_LAFFLESIA), giving fans an early taste of what's to come. The Linkcore embed suggests multiple platform availability at launch, so pre-orders and links should go live through standard Japanese digital retailers.</p>
<p>If you're after new Japanese rock with a darker, more theatrical edge, this is worth your attention. The single's French-influenced title and aesthetic positioning hint at the band's commitment to the Visual Kei tradition of blending Western influence with distinctly Japanese rock sensibilities.</p>
<p>**Grab the single when it drops on April 18th, 2026.** Check the preview video on their Twitter and keep an eye on the Linkcore widget for pre-order and streaming/purchase options as they become available. With momentum building from their debut, LAFFLESIA could be one of the interesting acts to watch in the Visual Kei underground this year.</p>
