Three Visual Kei veterans are joining forces to help lifelong bandgyaru and bandmen stay beautiful, healthy, and stage-ready—no matter their age. Tickets for their upcoming events go on general sale May 6th at noon.

Hiro (ex-ν[NEU]), DAISHI (Psycho le Cému vocalist), and SATOち (ex-MUCC) have quietly become pillars of the VK community's wellness movement. Each brings serious credentials: Hiro runs the private esthetic salon Dear Lily, DAISHI owns the private gym MIRO, and SATOち works as a trainer at MIRO. Rather than abandoning the Visual Kei ethos after their performing days, these three are channeling their scene roots into beauty and fitness expertise—proving that "lifetime active duty" isn't just a catchphrase.

Their collaboration kicks off with **"The Beautiful Visual Kei Talk"** on **May 24th in Harajuku, Tokyo** (Funny Space Harajuku). The trio will discuss why Visual Kei fans seem to age slower than everyone else, combining salon and gym wisdom in one hilarious, insightful session.

But there's more. Hiro is also running her **"Super Beauty Seminar for Lifetime Bandgyaru,"** with an expanded tour hitting multiple cities. After two shows in Tokyo on June 14th (Grace Valley Shibuya), the "Solo Travel Edition" rolls out to Shizuoka (July 19th), Sendai (August 2nd), Hokkaido (September 13th), Hiroshima (October 4th), and Fukuoka (November 15th).

Here's the kicker: **all tickets cost just ¥500—and include beauty/health-themed merchandise.** It's a deliberate move to make wellness accessible to everyone in the scene, not just the wealthy elite.

If you're serious about maintaining your lifestyle as a lifelong Visual Kei fan—or just want to hear three legends dish about staying gorgeous and fit—mark your calendar. General ticket sales open **May 6th at noon** via [Tiget](https://tiget.net/performers/214945).

Because your oshi would want you healthy enough to attend their reunion shows in 2045.
