<p>Sid guitarist Shinji is back with the second collection from his original fashion brand No Effect, and this drop proves he's just as thoughtful about design as he is about guitar work.</p>
<p>**About Sid:** For those unfamiliar, Sid is a Japanese Visual Kei band known for their dark, atmospheric sound and theatrical presentation. Shinji, the band's guitarist, has channeled his creative vision beyond music into a fashion venture that mirrors the VK ethos of authenticity and artistic expression.</p>
<p>No Effect operates on a straightforward philosophy: zero unnecessary effects, all natural materials. Rather than chasing trends, the brand celebrates raw craftsmanship and how pieces develop character over time. The second collection stays true to this vision while keeping everyday wearability at the forefront—think refined minimalism meets genuine substance.</p>
<p>**What's in the Drop:**</p>
<p>The new lineup features four carefully curated pieces. A pick-motif necklace in cow leather and brass doubles as a functional accessory holder. Wide-silhouette work pants balance elegance with durability. A lightweight shirt with cooling-contact technology offers breathability for warmer weather. And organic cotton 100% waffle-knit tank tops deliver comfort with excellent ventilation.</p>
<p>Every item carries Shinji's personal handwritten designs and messages throughout, meaning each piece gains deeper character the more you wear it—exactly what VK fans appreciate about brands that respect their audience's intelligence.</p>
<p>**Pre-Order Details:**</p>
<p>Orders open **April 20, 2026 at 6:00 PM JST** and run through April 30 at 11:59 PM. Shipping is expected around late August 2026. Note that cancellations aren't permitted after ordering.</p>
<p>Limited quantities apply to the shirts, pants, and tanks (pre-order production), while necklaces are also made-to-order. Buyers get access to exclusive in-person meetups or online talk sessions where Shinji personally stamps and hands over special gifts.</p>
<p>Head to **https://www.noeffect.net/** to shop, and follow the brand on Instagram (@no_effect_official) for updates. This is the kind of project that rewards VK fans who value artistry beyond the stage.</p>
