<p>The Benjamin are gearing up for a major release this spring with their new album *Beyond*, arriving in two formats for eager fans.</p>
<p>The record will hit digital platforms on **March 21, 2026**, with a physical CD release following on **March 29, 2026** at ¥3,500. For those unfamiliar with the band, The Benjamin are known for their dynamic blend of rock aesthetics and theatrical presentation, bringing energy and intricate songwriting to the Japanese rock underground.</p>
<p>The *Beyond* tracklist showcases a creative vision dominated by the songwriting of Miney, who helms 11 of the 14 compositions, with Mashoe contributing the remaining three. Notably, the tracks lean heavily into a thematic alphabet motif with Japanese titles—from "ボーダーライン" (Borderline) and "ブラッドオレンジ" (Blood Orange) to "ボストンバッグ" (Boston Bag) and "バンダナ" (Bandana). The English-language tracks "Bootleg Apples" and "Blunt Breath" provide stylistic variety, while the closer "Beyond the LOVE" ties directly to the album's title.</p>
<p>The album's lead single, "Ballade -Love is now-," already has a full music video in circulation. [Check it out on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bcAKSiOU34U) to get a taste of what's coming.</p>
<p>The deliberate thematic consistency across the tracklist suggests this isn't just a collection of songs—it's a carefully curated listening experience. Whether you're drawn to The Benjamin's theatrical edge or their rock-forward songwriting, *Beyond* promises to deliver both substance and style.</p>
<p>**Mark your calendars:** digital pre-orders should go live soon, and physical copies will be available through standard Japanese music retailers. Western fans may want to explore import options through usual channels.</p>
