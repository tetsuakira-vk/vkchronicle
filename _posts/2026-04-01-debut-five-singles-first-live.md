<p>A fresh face has emerged in the Visual Kei underground: **あの子の部屋のカレンデュラは** (stylized as "anocale") has officially formed and is already making waves with an ambitious digital release strategy.</p>
<p>The newly assembled four-piece consists of vocalist 瑠依 (Rui), guitarist 9, bassist 惟緒 (Io), and drummer support 水崎達彦 (Misaki Tatsuhiko)—a familiar name to VK devotees, as Tatsuhiko previously drummed for **1年4組 (Ichiyon)** and **Featuring School**. This pedigree suggests the band is tapping into established underground networks, which bodes well for their artistic direction.</p>
<p>**What to Expect**</p>
<p>Rather than the traditional single-at-a-time rollout, anocale is taking a bold approach: five digital singles will drop across streaming platforms, each with evocative titles that hint at the theatrical darkness VK fans crave:</p>
<p>- "春鬻ぐ、歌舞伎町" (Spring Selling, Kabukicho)</p>
<p>- "メンヘラコレクション" (Mental Health Collection)</p>
<p>- "黒百合の途" (Path of Black Lilies)</p>
<p>- "偏愛エチュード" (Biased Affection Etude)</p>
<p>- "ミスリード" (Mislead)</p>
<p>These titles alone suggest a band unafraid to embrace darker aesthetics and introspective, potentially anguished lyrical territory—classic VK hallmarks.</p>
<p>**Live Debut Incoming**</p>
<p>Mark your calendars: anocale will hold their first live performance on **June 1, 2026**. For Western fans, this is your heads-up to monitor their social channels for potential streaming announcements or international fan accessibility details.</p>
<p>The band has already begun distributing their music to streaming services as of April 8, 2026, so you can discover their sound immediately without waiting for the live event.</p>
<p>**Where to Follow**</p>
<p>Keep up with anocale's latest announcements on their [Twitter/X account](https://x.com/anocaleofficial), and check out their [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mg-J6Wq8WK0) where you'll find their streaming release announcement.</p>
<p>This is a band worth watching closely. Their deliberate, multi-single strategy and Tatsuhiko's drumming experience suggest serious artistic ambitions. Stream their work now and catch that June debut if you can.</p>
