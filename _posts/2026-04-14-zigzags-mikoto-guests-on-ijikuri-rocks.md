<p>After six years away, the vocalist of one of Japan's most theatrical Visual Kei acts is making a long-awaited return to the spotlight. Mikoto (-真天地開闢集団-ジグザグ) will guest on the 74th episode of *いじくりROCKS!* ("ijikuri ROCKS!"), the streaming show co-hosted by MUCC's Tatsurou and NoGoD's Danchō, airing **April 21, 2026 at 7:00 PM JST**.</p>
<p>This marks Mikoto's first in-studio appearance since the program's early days—a six-year gap that makes this a genuinely significant moment for fans. -真天地開闢集団-ジグザグ (literally "True Heaven-Earth Genesis Collective Zigzag") have been quietly building momentum. Last year they released the single **"P0WER -悪霊退散-"** ("P0WER -Evil Spirit Exorcism-"), which became the opening theme for the anime adaptation of *Hell Teacher Nube*. The band is currently in the middle of their nationwide tour **"2026 ＜47 Prefectures Leisurely Pilgrimage Purification ~Phase Two＞,"** bringing their unmistakable theatrical energy to every corner of Japan.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with Zigzag, they exemplify Visual Kei's commitment to spectacle and storytelling—their live shows are genuine events, brimming with elaborate stage design and conceptual depth. This appearance on *ijikuri ROCKS!* should give Western fans a rare opportunity to hear directly from Mikoto about the band's creative direction and current touring campaign.</p>
<p>The episode also features **NETH PRIERE CAIN**, a Nagoya-based Visual Kei outfit who gained traction on social media as "the Visual Kei band turning live venues into cotillions in the Reiwa era." They'll appear in the "Mr. JACK Cuts Through!" segment, curated by MUCC's YUKKE. This new-lineup version of NETH PRIERE CAIN should reveal where the band is headed next.</p>
<p>**Broadcast Details:**</p>
<p>- **Date:** April 21, 2026 | 7:00 PM JST</p>
<p>- **Hosts:** Tatsurou (MUCC), Danchō (NoGoD)</p>
<p>- **Platform:** Niconico Live Broadcast</p>
<p>- **Watch:** [https://live.nicovideo.jp/watch/lv350306125](https://live.nicovideo.jp/watch/lv350306125)</p>
<p>Catch this rare chance to connect with Zigzag's latest chapter—tune in and see what Mikoto has to share after all this time.</p>
