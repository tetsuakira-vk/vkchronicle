<p>Japanese Visual Kei act IRIS MONDO is gearing up to deliver fresh material this spring with the announcement of their new single "Beyond," set to drop on April 8, 2026.</p>
<p>IRIS MONDO has carved out a distinctive presence within the Visual Kei scene, blending heavy instrumentals with theatrical presentation and moody atmospherics. The band's visual identity—sharp aesthetics paired with dynamic stage presence—has earned them a dedicated following among Western VK fans seeking acts that balance sonic heaviness with the genre's signature artistic ambition.</p>
<p>While details about "Beyond" remain limited at this stage, the announcement comes with promotional imagery that hints at the band's continued commitment to striking visuals. Those unfamiliar with IRIS MONDO can get a taste of their style through their existing catalog; their track "勝ちにいこう" (Kachi ni Ikou, meaning "Let's Go for Victory") showcases their ability to merge aggressive riffing with memorable hooks—available on their official YouTube channel.</p>
<p>The single's release represents another milestone for a band that has remained consistent in the competitive Visual Kei landscape. With physical and digital releases typically following standard Japanese music industry channels, fans should watch for pre-order announcements on major retailers and the band's official social media.</p>
<p>For those who haven't yet discovered IRIS MONDO, "Beyond" offers an ideal entry point. Keep an eye on their Twitter (@IRIS_MONDO) for updates on tracklist details, lyrical themes, and any potential accompanying visuals or live performance announcements.</p>
<p>Mark your calendars for April 8, 2026, and prepare to venture "Beyond" with this rising Visual Kei force.</p>
