<p>Cloudy are ramping up their momentum with the announcement of their second full-length album and an ambitious nationwide tour spanning August through November 2026.</p>
<p>**できるだけ感動の方へ** (Dekiru Dake Kandō no Hō e) drops August 5, 2026 on the Cloudy label at ¥2,500. This marks the first CD release with the band's current lineup and arrives nearly two years after their debut album. The project compiles previously released digital singles, including "無責任な肯定を" (Musekinin na Kōtei wo), which won the RO JACK audition tournament last year, plus three consecutive monthly releases from early 2026: "サミダレ," "孤軍奮闘歌," and "捧ぐ."</p>
<p>The **2nd Album 'Dekiru Dake Kandō no Hō e' Release Tour** kicks off August 15 with a battle-of-the-bands show at Chiba LOOK, sweeping across eight additional regional venues through September. The tour then pivots to a one-man show circuit beginning October 1 at Tokyo's Shibuya WWW X—an added date after the November 22 Shibuya CLUB QUATTRO final sold out instantly.</p>
<p>Tour stops include major cities like Yokohama, Kyoto, Osaka, Nagoya, and Sapporo, giving fans nationwide a chance to experience the new material live. Whether you catch them on the battle circuit or their headline dates, this tour signals serious growth for the outfit.</p>
<p>**Store-exclusive pre-order bonuses** are available now: Tower Records offers original picks, Amazon Japan includes a mega-jacket, and other retailers provide exclusive stickers. Official pre-sales open April 4 at 10 PM JST through April 23 via e+.</p>
<p>If you haven't caught Cloudy yet, this album release and tour cycle is the perfect entry point. Head to their official site or e+ to secure your tickets—especially if you're eyeing one of the one-man shows.</p>
