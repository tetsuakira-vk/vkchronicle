LEX just dropped a darkly humorous new single that channels the desolate atmospherics of a sprawling fantasy game world. "ELDEN RING," released today, finds the band translating that sense of loneliness and escapism through their distinctively theatrical lens — painting solitary wandering across vast digital landscapes with both wit and shadow.

For those unfamiliar with LEX, the band has carved out a reputation for blending visual spectacle with introspective emotional depth, capturing that signature VK tension between beauty and melancholy. This new track appears to be another statement of their evolving sound, one that draws inspiration from interactive media while remaining unmistakably their own.

![LEX promotional image](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F06%2F10114121%2Fccw_lex26-208_Aphoto-819x1024.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

Beyond the single, LEX has announced something significant: their first-ever arena headline show. On October 30, 2026, they'll take over Tokyo's TOYOTA ARENA TOKYO for what promises to be a landmark evening in their career. The venue's scale reflects the band's growing presence and the devoted fanbase they've built.

![ELDEN RING cover art](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F06%2F10114132%2FELDEN-RING-JKT.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

Ticket information is now live. The third official pre-sale period runs from June 10 at noon through August 2 at 11:59 PM JST. Pricing breaks down as follows: Standing S area ¥13,000; Standing A area and designated A seating ¥9,900 (both tax included). Doors open at 6 PM, with the show starting at 7 PM.

To grab tickets and secure your spot, head directly to the official ticketing platform. Stream "ELDEN RING" across all major platforms via LEX's official distribution channels.
