<p>Zeke Deux is giving fans a visual feast with the upcoming release of *Music Video Collection IV*, arriving **May 19, 2026**.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with Zeke Deux, the band represents the experimental and avant-garde edge of Visual Kei—delivering theatrical compositions paired with striking visual presentations that push the genre's boundaries. This new DVD marks the fourth installment in their music video anthology series, a testament to the group's prolific creative output and their commitment to delivering compelling visual content alongside their music.</p>
<p>The DVD will feature **seven tracks**, including some standout numbers from their recent catalog:</p>
<p>1. Floraison</p>
<p>2. Glitter</p>
<p>3. Carnival</p>
<p>4. Flare</p>
<p>5. Another Wing</p>
<p>6. JaiL</p>
<p>7. Member Comments</p>
<p>That final bonus track—member comments—offers an intimate glimpse into the band's creative process and personalities, a nice touch for dedicated fans who want more than just the visuals.</p>
<p>The announcement also mentions **Premium Ticket Benefits**, suggesting special perks for early adopters or fans purchasing through official channels. While full details on those benefits haven't been spelled out yet, it's worth keeping an eye on Zeke Deux's official social channels for complete information.</p>
<p>For Western VK fans, music video collections like this are essential purchases. They showcase not only the band's sonic evolution but also their visual direction—something fundamental to Visual Kei's identity. Each installment in this series offers a window into how Zeke Deux continues to refine their theatrical approach to rock music.</p>
<p>If you've been following the band or are curious about Zeke Deux's more experimental side of Visual Kei, mark your calendars. The **May 19, 2026** release date is coming up, and this collection is sure to be a must-have for collectors.</p>
