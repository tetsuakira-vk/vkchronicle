The legendary heavy metal outfit Damian Hamada's Creatures is ramping up momentum with a lyric video premiere and an upcoming headline show that metal fans won't want to miss.

On June 6, 2026 at 21:00 JST, the band will premiere an animated lyric video for their new single "ヴァンパイアの娘" (Vampire's Daughter). The page will officially open at midnight that same day, giving fans immediate access to the visuals that accompany the track. According to the announcement, the animation and lyrics are designed to deepen listeners' understanding and connection to the composition.

![Vampire's Daughter single artwork](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F%25E3%2580%258C%25E3%2583%25B4%25E3%2582%25A1%25E3%2583%25B3%25E3%2583%2591%25E3%2582%25A4%25E3%2582%25A2%25E3%2581%25AE%25E5%25A8%2598%25E3%2580%258D%25E3%2582%25B5%25E3%2583%25A0%25E3%2583%258D%25E3%2582%25A4%25E3%2583%25ABsmall-580x270.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

For those unfamiliar, Damian Hamada's Creatures represents the return of a legendary figure: Damian Hamada, the original architect behind cult icons Seikima II (聖飢魔II), who penned early classics like "蠟人形の館" (The Wax Museum). After 35 years away, he's reassembled with a fresh lineup and a mission to reshape the metal landscape. Vocalist Sakura Ishadou brings X-Factor credentials, while the current instrumental lineup features RENO Faust, AKS KAZUMA, Lilith Ishinose, and KAZAMI Crowley. The band's catalogue of "scriptures" (albums)—including the *Old Testament Demon Bible* volumes and *Demon Realm Art Museum*—has consistently topped domestic metal streaming charts globally.

The single "ヴァンパイアの娘" arrives ahead of a major live event: **Damian Hamada's Creatures "THE WORST 10"** on August 15, 2026, at Veats Shibuya in Tokyo. Doors open at 17:00, with the show starting at 17:30. General admission standing tickets cost ¥7,000 (plus a separate venue charge), with ticket applications limited to four per person. Children under six are not permitted; ages six and up require tickets.
