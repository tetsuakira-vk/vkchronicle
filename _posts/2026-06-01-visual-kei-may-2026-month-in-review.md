<h2>VK Chronicle's May 2026 Month in Review</h2>

<p>May proved to be Visual Kei's most explosive month in years—a whirlwind of legendary comebacks, surprise debuts, and the kind of scene-wide momentum that reminds us why we fell in love with this genre in the first place.</p>
<!--more-->

<h3>Legendary Returns & Comebacks</h3>

<p>The month opened with seismic news that had the entire fanbase in freefall: <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/d-espairsray-return-with-first-single-in-16-years/">D'ESPAIRSRAY returned with their first single in 16 years</a>, cementing May as the month of the impossible comeback. Hot on their heels came equally thrilling news that <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/corpse-another-person-reunites-after-30-year-hiatus/">CORPSE ANOTHER PERSON reunited after a staggering 30-year hiatus</a>—a jaw-dropping moment for longtime fans of Japanese rock history. Meanwhile, <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/kisaki-celebrates-50th-birthday-with-album-final-masquerade-/">KISAKI celebrated his 50th birthday with a new album and his final Masquerade tour</a>, marking a poignant milestone for one of Visual Kei's most influential figures.</p>

<h3>Major Releases & Studio Output</h3>

<p>The release schedule was relentless. <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/dir-en-grey-s-mortal-downer-embraces-despair-in-12th-album/">Dir En Grey's "Mortal Downer" embraced despair in their 12th album</a>, with the accompanying <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/dir-en-grey-drop-mobs-video-from-mortal-downer-album/">"MOBS" video dropping shortly after</a>. <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/drugs-unveil-new-look-with-8th-single-akuma/">DRUGS unveiled a new look with their 8th single "Akuma"</a>, while <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/iq99-return-with-third-mini-album-wonder-flight/">IQ99 returned with their third mini album "Wonder Flight"</a>. Emerging acts like <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/kuromitsu-debut-mv-out-now/">Kuromitsu debuted their MV "君のハーフツインを食べたい"</a> and <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/fat-m-launches-official-site-ahead-of-may-30-debut-show/">Fatüm launched their official site ahead of their May 30 debut show</a>, signaling fresh creative energy entering the scene.</p>

<h3>Tours, Festivals & Live Events</h3>

<p><a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/masked-2026-day-2-d-espairsray-royz-close-legendary-festival/">MASKED 2026 closed with D'ESPAIRSRAY and Royz headlining Day 2</a>, delivering an unforgettable festival moment. <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/luna-sea-announce-arena-shows-following-hometown-tour-opener/">LUNA SEA announced arena shows following their hometown tour opener</a>, though the month carried somber weight as <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/luna-sea-honor-drummer-shnya-at-emotional-farewell-service/">the band honored drummer Shnya at an emotional farewell service</a>. Meanwhile, <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/merry-mucc-and-reunite-for-daisuke-matsuri-one-final-festiva/">Merry, MUCC, and 蜉蝣 reunited for "Daisuke Matsuri," one final festival</a>. Tour announcements flooded in: <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/age-factory-enth-paledusk-launch-goblin-zepp-tour-2026/">Age Factory, ENTH, and Paledusk launched the GOBLIN Zepp Tour 2026</a>, and <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/reiwa-no-sofubi-tour-unites-four-vk-acts-for-soft-visual-aes/">"Reiwa no Sofubi" united four VK acts for a soft visual aesthetic tour</a>.</p>

<h3>Anime, Media & Cultural Moments</h3>

<p><a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/yamikumo-hatred-lands-tv-tokyo-ed-theme/">闇雲's "Hatred" landed a TV Tokyo ED theme</a>, while <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/nelke-s-greedy-becomes-japanese-engineering-institute-cm-son/">NELKE's "Greedy!!" became a Japanese Engineering Institute CM song</a>. <a href="https://vkchronicle.com/2026/05/glay-s-hisashi-talks-ragnarok-anime-season-3-collab/">GLAY's HISASHI discussed the Ragnarok Anime Season 3 collaboration</a>, highlighting Visual Kei's expanding cultural footprint.</p>
