<p>Visual Kei veterans Royz are gearing up for their next chapter with the announcement of their 30th maxi-single, *PHOENIXXXX-フェニックス-* (Phoenix), arriving on **June 27, 2026**.</p>
<p>The milestone release marks another significant moment in Royz's ongoing career as one of Japan's most consistent Visual Kei acts. Since their debut in the early 2010s, the band has maintained a dedicated following both domestically and internationally with their signature blend of heavy instrumentation, dramatic aesthetics, and theatrical presentation—hallmarks of the Visual Kei movement.</p>
<p>The phoenix imagery in the title carries symbolic weight; a creature that rises from ashes and is reborn. For a band with Royz's longevity in an ever-shifting music landscape, the metaphor feels apt. After nearly a decade and a half of releases, they continue to evolve their sound while staying true to the theatrical darkness that defines their identity.</p>
<p>While specific track listings and further details remain limited at this stage, fans should expect the polished production and dynamic songwriting that characterize recent Royz output. The band has consistently delivered strong singles alongside deep cuts that showcase their range—from atmospheric ballads to crushing heavy passages.</p>
<p>For Western fans who may be less familiar with Royz, they represent an important part of the modern Visual Kei ecosystem: a band serious about their craft, uncompromising in their aesthetic vision, and committed to the long game. They're neither chasing mainstream trends nor resting on nostalgia; instead, they're steadily building their legacy one release at a time.</p>
<p>Keep an eye on [Royz's official Twitter](https://x.com/Royz_official) for more details about *PHOENIXXXX* in the coming months. Pre-orders will likely become available through major Japanese retailers closer to the release date. If you've been sleeping on Royz, now's a perfect time to catch up before the new single drops.</p>
