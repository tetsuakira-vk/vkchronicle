Guitarist Mitsuki from both Sadie and The THIRTEEN has announced a major summer event centered around his fashion brand THE MALEVOLENT EDUCATION—and it's shaping up to be unmissable for devoted fans.

Since launching in 2015, THE MALEVOLENT EDUCATION has carved out a distinctive aesthetic within the Visual Kei fashion ecosystem, building a devoted following with each carefully curated seasonal collection. This August marks the release of the brand's 17th collection, titled "KILL THE SCAPEGOAT"—a provocative theme that promises bold artistic direction. The exhibition and sales event will run August 1st at Sakura-shinmachi NEIGHBOR in Tokyo's Setagaya ward, with doors open from 2:00 PM to 4:30 PM (sales until 4:00 PM).

![THE MALEVOLENT EDUCATION Collection Poster](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2FMZK_%25E3%2583%2597%25E3%2583%25A9%25E3%2582%25B0%25E3%2583%25AC%25E3%2582%25B9202608-580x827.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

Those purchasing ¥30,000 or more in merchandise will receive a one-on-one meet-and-greet ticket plus a 2-shot Cheki (instant photo) with Mitsuki—an increasingly rare opportunity in today's live music landscape.

**THE PLUGLESS Returns for Fan Club Only Experience**

The same evening brings "THE PLUGLESS M's LIMITED vol.17," an intimate fan club exclusive event. Taking place at the same venue with doors at 6:00 PM and music starting at 6:30 PM, this special setlist strips away the band format to focus on vocals, piano, and conversation. The ¥5,500 ticket price reflects the exclusivity—CLUB13 membership is mandatory to purchase, and your member number must be entered during checkout.

Tickets go live May 22nd at 10:00 PM via the official shop. Given the limited capacity and member-only access, expect these to move quickly.

For full details, ticket links, and collection previews, head to the official shop and CLUB13 member portal. This is the kind of intimate, artist-driven event that defines the best of Visual Kei culture—don't sleep on it.
