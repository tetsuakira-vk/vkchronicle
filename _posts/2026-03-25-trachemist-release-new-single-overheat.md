<p>Visual Kei outfit TRACHEMIST is gearing up for a fresh sonic assault with their upcoming single "Overheat Man" (オーバーヒートマン), set to drop on **May 13, 2026**.</p>
<p>The three-track release showcases the band's signature theatrical intensity across a diverse tracklist. Leading the charge is the title track "Overheat Man," alongside "Good Morning!" (おそようございます!) and the intriguingly titled "Golden Wind"—a lineup that promises variety while maintaining the group's energetic aesthetic.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with TRACHEMIST, the band has established themselves within the Japanese Visual Kei scene with their bold visual presentation and dynamic musical arrangements. They blend the theatrical elements core to VK with modern rock sensibilities, creating a sound that appeals to both longtime genre devotees and newer listeners discovering the movement.</p>
<p>The single artwork, which features the band's characteristic striking visual identity, has already generated buzz within the community. The release follows the band's continued activity in Japan's underground and mid-tier rock circuit, where they've built a dedicated fanbase through consistent touring and recorded output.</p>
<p>This is an excellent opportunity for Western VK fans to experience TRACHEMIST's latest work. The band represents the kind of artistry that keeps Visual Kei thriving—bands unafraid to experiment while honoring the movement's core principles of visual spectacle and musical ambition.</p>
<p>**Mark your calendars for May 13, 2026.** Pre-orders and additional details should become available through the band's official channels. Keep an eye on their **[Twitter account](https://x.com/TRCM_official)** for the latest updates and possibly some behind-the-scenes material as the release date approaches.</p>
<p>If you haven't experienced TRACHEMIST yet, this single is the perfect entry point into their world.</p>
