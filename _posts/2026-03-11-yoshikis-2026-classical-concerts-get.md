<p>If you've been trying to snag tickets to YOSHIKI's highly anticipated classical concerts and hit a payment wall, you're not alone—and the good news is you're getting a second chance.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar, YOSHIKI is the legendary drummer and composer best known as the founding member of X Japan, the pioneering Japanese heavy metal band that essentially created Visual Kei. Beyond his work in metal, YOSHIKI has cultivated a prestigious career as a classical composer and conductor, blending orchestral arrangements with his signature dramatic intensity. His classical concerts are intimate, prestigious affairs that sell out quickly.</p>
<p>**What Went Wrong?**</p>
<p>The initial ticket lottery for YOSHIKI CLASSICAL 2026 experienced significant system failures, particularly affecting overseas applicants trying to complete purchases. Recognizing the unfairness to fans locked out by technical glitches, organizer UDO Artists has extended the advance lottery period and pushed back the general on-sale date to level the playing field.</p>
<p>**Here's What You Need to Know:**</p>
<p>The rescheduled three-night run takes place **April 3–5, 2026** at **Tokyo Garden Theater**. Standard tickets are ¥18,000 (approximately $120 USD); VIP packages run ¥100,000.</p>
<p>The **new lottery application window opens February 10 at 12:00 PM Japan Time and closes February 25 at 11:59 PM**. Credit card payment is now mandatory for all advance applications. Multiple entry points exist—including YOSHIKI Plus, YOSHIKI SCARLET ARMY, international fan access, and various Japanese credit card programs.</p>
<p>**Lawson Ticket advance sales** run February 26 through March 15, while **general tickets go on sale March 21 at 12:00 PM Japan Time**.</p>
<p>International fans can collect tickets on the day of the event, making this genuinely accessible for Western attendees willing to travel. Remember: all lottery selections require full payment with no partial cancellations permitted.</p>
<p>For full details and direct entry links, visit **https://www.yoshiki.net/news/yoshiki-classical-2026**. If you missed the first window, this extended period is your shot—YOSHIKI's classical concerts are unmissable.</p>
