Two distinct artistic visions are set to collide this autumn. Verde/ (the solo project of Shou) and ASAGI have announced a special two-man live titled "浅き宵に翠満ちて" (An Evening of Pale Hue and Verdant Fullness) taking place October 12, 2026 at Takadanobaba CLUB PHASE in Tokyo.

The event promises a rare meeting of two storytellers who have long shaped the Visual Kei landscape through fundamentally different aesthetic approaches. ASAGI is renowned for crafting sprawling narrative worlds—drawing from vampire lore, yokai mythology, and gothic fantasy—and translating them into haunting, cinematic soundscapes. Verde/, by contrast, centers on Shou's intricate, sensual compositions that explore delicate emotional territories and push experimental boundaries. Despite their divergent paths, both artists share an uncompromising commitment to beauty and world-building.

The supporting lineup reflects the caliber of this collaboration. ASAGI's band will feature HIRO (La'cryma Christi) on guitar, ギル (Angelo / ex-Vidoll) on guitar, 亜季 (Sadie / AXESSORY) on bass, and HIROKI (D) on drums. Verde/'s ensemble includes 優介 and 燿 (both from 摩天楼オペラ) plus KEN'ICHI on drums. This roster represents some of the most respected session musicians in the Japanese underground scene.

**Event Details:**

Date: October 12, 2026 (Monday, National Holiday)  
Venue: Takadanobaba CLUB PHASE  
Doors: 5:00 PM / Start: 5:30 PM  
All standing

**Ticket Information:**

Pre-reservation begins July 18, 2026 via PIA (https://w.pia.jp/t/verde-asagi-t/). VIP tickets (¥11,000) include priority entry and an exclusive 2-shot photo with Shou and ASAGI. Standard general admission (¥6,600) is available from August 8 onward. Children under 6 are not permitted; those 6 and older require individual tickets.

For more details and ticket links, visit Verde/ at https://lit.link/RoomEvergreen or ASAGI at https://www.god-child-records.com/asagi/. Inquiries can be directed to official.verde1210@gmail.com or asagi-soloworks@god-child-records.com.
