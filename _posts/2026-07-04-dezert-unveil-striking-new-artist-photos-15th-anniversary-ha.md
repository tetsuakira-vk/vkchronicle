DEZERT has revealed a bold new artist photograph that strips away visual excess to showcase the raw presence of the band's current four-member lineup. Rendered entirely in monochrome, the minimalist aesthetic marks a striking visual statement as the Tokyo-based Visual Kei act prepares for a significant milestone.

![DEZERT new artist photo 2026](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F07%2F04154331%2F%25E3%2580%2590%25E2%2580%25BB7%25E6%259C%25884%25E6%2597%25A520%25E6%2599%2582%25E8%25A7%25A3%25E7%25A6%2581%25E3%2580%2591A%25E5%2586%2599DEZERT_Main_2026.07.04.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

Alongside the new photography, DEZERT has announced comprehensive details for their **15th Anniversary Hall Tour 2026: "Welcome To My Beginning."** The tour will traverse three major Japanese cities across September 2026, hitting Osaka, Nagoya, and Tokyo with three separate shows.

The tour kicks off on **September 12** at NHK Osaka Hall, followed by a Nagoya date at Niterra Nihon Tokushu Togyou Civic Hall Village Hall on **September 19**, and concludes in Tokyo at Kanadevia Hall (Tokyo Dome City Hall) on **September 27**. All shows feature a 16:30 or 16:45 doors with 17:30 start times.

![Tour details and ticketing info](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F06%2F20151530%2F%25EF%25BC%2591%25EF%25BC%2590%25EF%25BC%2592%25EF%25BC%2594-35.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

General admission tickets are priced at ¥7,700 (tax included), with all seats reserved. Note that the Tokyo show requires an additional beverage charge upon entry, and attendees must be at least three years old (tickets required from age three and up). Resale for profit is strictly prohibited.

The **official fanclub "Himawari-Kai" lottery presale** is currently open and represents the fastest access to tickets. The presale runs from July 4 at 20:00 through July 13 at 23:00 JST, with payment due between July 17 and July 20. Up to four tickets per performance are available per fanclub member, and non-members can be added as companions. All tickets are processed through Lawson and distributed via Smart Ticket only.

If you're unfamiliar with DEZERT, the band has maintained a dedicated following throughout their 15-year career with their distinctive blend of aggressive Visual Kei aesthetics and emotionally resonant songwriting. This anniversary tour marks an important celebration of their longevity in the scene.

**Head to the official DEZERT website at dezert.jp to register for the fanclub presale and secure your spot at these hometown hall shows.** International fans should note that Japanese residency or a Japanese phone number may be required for ticket registration—consider reaching out to fan communities for support navigating the ticketing system.
