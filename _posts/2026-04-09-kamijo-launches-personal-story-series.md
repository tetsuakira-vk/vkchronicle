<p>KAMIJO, the theatrical mastermind behind the iconic symphonic metal act Versailles and a true pillar of the international Visual Kei scene, is inviting fans into his inner world through an exclusive new article series titled "LA VIE EN ROSE" (Life in Pink).</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with KAMIJO's legacy: he's the visionary frontman who helped define modern Visual Kei's operatic, classical-influenced sound. Versailles, his project that debuted in 2007, became one of the most celebrated symphonic rock acts to emerge from Japan, blending elaborate Gothic aesthetics with orchestral arrangements and virtuosic musicianship. Beyond Versailles, KAMIJO has remained a creative force, collaborating across multiple projects and consistently pushing the boundaries of what Visual Kei can express artistically.</p>
<p>In this new series, KAMIJO steps into the role of personal storyteller, sharing candid reflections on the formative moments and influences that shaped his artistic identity. Through original articles written in his own voice, he'll revisit his early days in the scene, the artists and sounds that inspired him, and the defining memories that built the foundation for his legendary career.</p>
<p>This kind of intimate behind-the-scenes content is rare and invaluable for longtime fans and newcomers alike. Rather than relying on interviews conducted by others, KAMIJO is controlling his own narrative—sharing unfiltered insights into what it took to become one of Visual Kei's most distinctive and influential voices.</p>
<p>Chapter 1 has already launched, and with a full series ahead, this is the perfect opportunity to deepen your understanding of one of the scene's most important figures. Whether you've followed KAMIJO since his Versailles debut or you're just discovering his work, "LA VIE EN ROSE" promises to offer perspectives you won't find anywhere else.</p>
<p>Head over to the full article to dive into Chapter 1, and keep an eye out for upcoming installments of this fascinating personal chronicle.</p>
