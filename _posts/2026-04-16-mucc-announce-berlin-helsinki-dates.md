<p>Gothic metalcore legends MUCC are bringing their dark, atmospheric sound to European stages this summer as part of an expanded festival circuit. The Japanese Visual Kei pioneers have confirmed two headline dates surrounding their appearance at B7Fest, the newly launched Visual Kei festival taking place in Paris.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar, MUCC has been a cornerstone of the Visual Kei scene since the late 1990s, blending heavy guitars with haunting melodies and deeply theatrical presentations. Known for their conceptual albums and intense live performances, the band continues to push boundaries within the genre while maintaining a dedicated international fanbase.</p>
<p>**Tour Details:**</p>
<p>The confirmed European dates are:</p>
<p>- **9 July 2026** – Berlin, Germany @ Kesselhaus</p>
<p>- **13 July 2026** – Helsinki, Finland @ Ääniwalli</p>
<p>Ticket sales open **23 April 2026 at 12:00 CET**. This gives fans just over three months' notice to plan travel and accommodation—essential if you're hoping to catch MUCC in either city.</p>
<p>The Berlin show marks a return to Germany's capital venue, while the Helsinki date offers Scandinavian fans a rare opportunity to experience the band live. Both cities have historically supported Visual Kei acts, so expect dedicated audiences and an electric atmosphere.</p>
<p>If you're planning to catch MUCC at multiple European venues this summer—including B7Fest in Paris—this tour leg makes a regional run feasible for Western fans. The band's live show is not to be missed; their combination of technical musicianship, visual presentation, and raw emotion creates an immersive experience that simply doesn't translate to recordings.</p>
<p>**Next Steps:**</p>
<p>Mark your calendars for the 23 April ticket announcement. Given MUCC's profile and the limited venue capacities in both cities, these shows will likely sell quickly. Follow B7's official channels and MUCC's social media for pre-sale details and any additional European dates that may be announced.</p>
