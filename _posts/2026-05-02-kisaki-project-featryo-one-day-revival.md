<p>One of Visual Kei's most respected session projects is making a rare comeback. KISAKI PROJECT feat.ryo will reunite for a special one-day event, bringing together some of the scene's most skilled musicians for what promises to be an unmissable performance.</p>
<p>The project centers on ryo, a vocalist with an impressive resume spanning D'ELSQUEL, 9GOATS BLACK OUT, HOLLOWGRAM, DÄLLE, and H.U.G. He'll be joined by KISAKI on bass—a musician best known for his work with Phantasmagoria and MIRAGE—alongside support guitar from HIZAKI, the virtuoso formerly of Versailles, SULFURIC ACID, Schwardix Marvally, and Jupiter. Completing the lineup are guitarist Iyoda Kohei from THE GENIUS ORCHESTRATION and drummer Ryota Moritani (ex.GLARD).</p>
<p>This is the kind of all-star collaboration that Visual Kei fans live for: seasoned musicians from beloved bands uniting for something special. KISAKI's session projects have consistently attracted top-tier talent, and this lineup is no exception.</p>
<p>The event will also feature an impressive supporting bill. Beyond KISAKI PROJECT feat.ryo, fans can expect performances from NEGA, defspiral, THE BEETHOVEN, ClearVeil, Phobia, Hikari Shiina, and NETH PRIERE CAIN. This is a packed card that caters to various Visual Kei and experimental rock aesthetics.</p>
<p>For Western fans, these one-off project events can be tricky to access, but they're worth investigating if you have connections in Japan or can coordinate with local VK communities. The combination of ryo's distinctive vocal presence and HIZAKI's technical prowess alone makes this essential viewing for Versailles and 9GOATS BLACK OUT enthusiasts.</p>
<p>Keep an eye on the official KISAKI Twitter account for further details, including exact dates, venue information, and ticket availability. This is the kind of special event that defines the Japanese Visual Kei underground—don't sleep on it.</p>
