Japanese rock outfit COPES is stepping up their game with a major-label signing and an ambitious nationwide tour on the horizon. The band has officially joined No Big Deal Records, a roster that already houses some of the country's most exciting contemporary acts including 04 Limited Sazabys, Wienners, and Neckrogue Talking.

To mark this new chapter, COPES will release their second mini album, *WAO!!*, on September 9th, 2026. Details about the release are still to come, but expect the same energetic, hook-driven rock sensibility the band has built their reputation on.

![Tour announcement flyer](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F06%2F28172157%2F%25E3%2583%2584%25E3%2582%25A2%25E3%2583%25BC%25E5%2591%258A%25E7%259F%25A5%25E3%2583%2595%25E3%2583%25A9%25E3%2582%25A4%25E3%2583%25A4%25E3%2583%25BC.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

The band are also launching a major touring cycle, *COPES presents "WAO!! tour"*, hitting 13 venues across Japan starting October 2nd. Each show will feature guest acts—lineups to be announced. The tour kicks off in Chiba on October 2nd and wraps up in Tokyo's Daikanyama on January 23rd, 2027, with stops in major cities including Takamatsu, Kyoto, Kanazawa, Yokohama, Sendai, Fukuoka, Hiroshima, Osaka, Nagoya, Sapporo, and Kobe.

Pre-sale tickets went live on June 28th via Ticket Pia at ¥3,300 (plus drink charge). If you're keen to catch COPES on this expanded run, now's the time to secure your spot—these intimate rock shows tend to fill fast.

For the latest on *WAO!!* and full tour details, head to COPES' official channels and keep an eye on No Big Deal Records' announcements. This is a band worth watching as they enter an exciting new phase of their career.
