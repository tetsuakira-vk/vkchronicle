<p>After marking their 20th anniversary with a final celebratory show at Tokyo Garden Theater in December, lynch. is stepping into year 21 with major announcements: a brand new studio album and an extensive national tour.</p>
<p>**CLIMAX arrives this summer**</p>
<p>*CLIMAX* drops July 1st, 2026—their first full-length original album in over three years (since 2023's *REBORN*). This marks their 12th studio outing. The album title itself carries weight: "climax" signals a peak moment, a crescendo. What concept and emotional depth the band has poured into the record remains to be revealed, though we can expect their signature evolving sound to push boundaries once again.</p>
<p>The band's new artist visual perfectly captures the album's aesthetic: members standing composed amid a decaying Western mansion, bathed in shadows yet pierced by light. It's quintessential lynch.—dark, theatrical, and visually arresting.</p>
<p>**Two edition formats available**</p>
<p>The Limited Edition (¥6,600 / $USD ~45) includes CD + Blu-ray in special packaging. A Standard Edition CD (¥3,500 / ~$24) also drops the same day. Both qualify for early-bird perks: pre-orders through King Records' official shop by May 31st net a B2 tour poster as an exclusive bonus. Tower Records will also stock exclusive items and host signing events across three cities (Shinjuku, Umeda, and Nagoya in July–September).</p>
<p>**The "IGNITE THE CLIMAX" Tour**</p>
<p>lynch. embarks on an 18-show nationwide trek titled **＜IGNITE THE CLIMAX＞** (roughly: "set the climax ablaze"). Kicking off July 12th in Osaka, the tour spans summer and autumn, hitting major cities from Hokkaido to Fukuoka, including a Zepp Shinjuku date on August 31st. Full ticket details arrive soon.</p>
<p>**Why you should care**</p>
<p>lynch. has always occupied a unique niche in Visual Kei—theatrical yet introspective, with a visual identity as compelling as their sonic palette. This album cycle signals they're not resting on legacy; they're pushing forward into new creative territory. Fans should mark their calendars and watch for ticket pre-sale announcements.</p>
<p>Check their official site and X account for updates, and grab pre-orders now to secure early bonuses.</p>
