Misui's main composer is stepping into the spotlight with a solo venture. Amane, the band's principal songwriter and guitarist, will release his debut solo single *Cor et Acus* on June 12th during his birthday one-man show at Ikebukuro's BlackHole venue—a day before his actual birthday on June 13th.

For those unfamiliar with Misui, the Tokyo-based act has built a reputation as one of Visual Kei's most compelling modern projects, combining technical musicianship with emotionally resonant songwriting. Their commitment to celebrating member milestones has become a hallmark; birthday shows are never routine affairs, and Amane's bash promises no exception.

![Misui performs at their birthday show](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2Fph1N7A6822-580x388.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

What makes this release particularly noteworthy is the shift in focus. While Amane is celebrated for crafting Misui's vocal tracks, *Cor et Acus* puts his aggressive, technically demanding guitar work front and center. The single showcases hard, symphonic, and emo-melodic songwriting—a different beast entirely from his band compositions. A brief teaser is available via the official channels, giving fans a taste of what's to come.

*Cor et Acus* arrives as a CD-R package bundled with a mini photo book, priced at ¥2,000. Crucially, it's only available at the venue or through limited online sales, so attending the live performance is the most direct route to securing a copy.

The "Amane Birthday One-Man *心臓に針を落とす。*" (Dropping Needles on the Heart) show kicks off at 6:00 PM on June 12th. General admission costs ¥4,000 in advance or ¥4,500 on the day, plus venue fees. The bill will feature Misui across both stages, and Amane will naturally perform the new single live.

![Misui performs live](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2Fph1N7A6886-580x388.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

Beyond this, keep an eye on Amane's appearances as a support guitarist for Crimson Shiva's limited reunion show on June 22nd in Shibuya—a chance to witness his technical prowess in a different context.

For tickets and full details, visit the official Misui website and social channels. You can also purchase tickets directly through the e-plus link. Stream and follow the band on all major platforms to stay updated on upcoming releases and tour dates.
