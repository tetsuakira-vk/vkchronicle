<p>A fresh supergroup has emerged from the Visual Kei underground, and the roster is stacked with familiar faces. ANCIENT MYTH has officially announced its complete lineup, bringing together experienced musicians from across the scene for what promises to be an intriguing new chapter.</p>
<p>Fronting the project is **Michal** on vocals, formerly of Mandylion and known by the codename Wingless. On guitar, **kaya** from kNeon brings technical prowess, while **Hal** of Ethereal Sin commands the keyboard. The rhythm section pairs **Raimu**—fresh from his time in DiAMiLiON and also a kNeon member—on bass with **Shibuki** from Omelas on drums.</p>
<p>What makes ANCIENT MYTH particularly intriguing is how it bridges different corners of the Visual Kei community. Raimu's addition to the lineup marks an exciting next move after his work with DiAMiLiON, and his presence alongside such a cohesive ensemble suggests this is more than just a casual side project. The combination of Michal's vocal experience, kaya's guitar work, and Hal's atmospheric keyboard textures hints at a sound that could balance melody with darker, theatrical undertones.</p>
<p>For Western fans still getting acquainted with Japan's underground rock scene, projects like this are where genuine innovation happens. While major Visual Kei acts dominate international attention, these supergroups—formed by respected session and touring musicians—often push creative boundaries in ways the mainstream never does. The cross-pollination of members from multiple bands creates unpredictable chemistry.</p>
<p>Details about their debut release, first live performance, and official sound remain sparse for now, but ANCIENT MYTH's social media is already live. If you're keen to follow their journey from day one, their **Twitter** (@ancientmyth) is the place to stay tuned.</p>
<p>This is the kind of project worth keeping on your radar. Keep your eyes and ears open for upcoming announcements—these lineups rarely disappoint.</p>
