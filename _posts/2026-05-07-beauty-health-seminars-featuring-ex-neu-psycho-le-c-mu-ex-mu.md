Three Visual Kei scene veterans are joining forces to bring health and beauty education to the fanbase—and they're making it ridiculously affordable.

Hīro (ex-ν[NEU]), owner and esthetician of the private salon DearLily, has been championing the "lifetime active" philosophy within the VK community through beauty expertise. Now she's teaming up with DAISHI (Psycho le Cému vocalist and owner of the private gym MIRO) and SATOchi (ex-MUCC, MIRO trainer) to host a series of events bridging physical wellness, mental health, and the passion that keeps Visual Kei fans going strong.

**The Main Event**

On **May 24** at FunnySpace Harajuku in Tokyo, the trio will host *"The Beautiful Visual Kei Talk"*—a cheeky reference to their combined age of approximately 150 years. Expect frank discussion about why Visual Kei musicians seem to age remarkably well, drawing on their combined expertise in skincare, fitness, and lifestyle.

**The Nationwide Tour**

Hīro's signature "Super Beauty Seminar for Lifelong Band Girls" expands nationally this year. After a two-part session in Tokyo on **June 14** (at Grace Valley Shibuya), she's taking the show on the road:

- **Shizuoka**: July 19
- **Sendai**: August 2
- **Hokkaido**: September 13
- **Hiroshima**: October 4
- **Fukuoka**: November 15

**Tickets & Access**

Here's the brilliant part: all events are just **¥500 ($3 USD)** per ticket, and includes a beauty/wellness goodie bag. General ticket sales begin **May 6 at noon JST** via [tiget.net/performers/214945](https://tiget.net/performers/214945) on a first-come, first-served basis.

The affordability isn't accidental—it reflects the organizers' genuine desire to make health and beauty accessible, not exclusive. Whether you're a lifelong band girl planning your 100-year-old concert tour wardrobe or simply curious about how the VK scene stays eternally fabulous, this is your moment. Tickets are moving fast, so don't sleep on this.
