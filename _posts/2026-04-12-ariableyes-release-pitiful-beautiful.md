<p>Ariabl'eyeS are bringing the gothic theatrics with a stunning new full-length album arriving this spring. *惨美なるブラッディドール* (Pitiful Beautiful Bloody Doll) hits shelves on April 26, 2026, and it's packed with the symphonic darkness the band has become known for.</p>
<p>If you're unfamiliar with Ariabl'eyeS, they're a Japanese symphonic metal outfit with strong Visual Kei aesthetics—think elaborate costumes, theatrical presentation, and ornate musical arrangements that blend classical elements with heavy rock. They've built a dedicated following in the Western Visual Kei community for their cinematic approach to the genre, creating immersive sonic narratives that feel like stepping into a gothic fever dream.</p>
<p>**The Tracklist:**</p>
<p>The album opens with "Prelude –Bloody Doll–" before launching into "Danse Macabre," immediately setting a darkly elegant tone. Standout tracks include "螺旋の玻璃" (Spiral Glass), "狂想のイヴ" (Mad Eve), the title track "惨美なるブラッディドール," and "冥き月のワルツ" (Dark Moon Waltz). Each song title hints at the gothic narrative woven throughout the release.</p>
<p>A nice touch: the album includes instrumental versions of all six main tracks, giving listeners both the full vocal experience and stripped-down arrangements that showcase the band's instrumental prowess.</p>
<p>**Get A Preview:**</p>
<p>You can already check out an XFD (extra fast demo—a Japanese music industry tradition of brief track previews) on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L-QK58XL-EA) to get a taste of what's coming.</p>
<p>Whether you're a longtime Ariabl'eyeS fan or a symphonic metal enthusiast looking for your next obsession, *惨美なるブラッディドール* promises to deliver the theatrical darkness Visual Kei does best. Mark April 26 on your calendar and prepare for an auditory descent into beautiful despair.</p>
