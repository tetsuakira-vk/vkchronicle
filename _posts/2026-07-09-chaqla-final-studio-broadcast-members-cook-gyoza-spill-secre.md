After six months of intimate studio conversations, CHAQLA. is bringing their regular live broadcast series to a symbolic close—and they're doing it over homemade dumplings.

For those unfamiliar with CHAQLA., the band cuts a striking figure in today's Visual Kei landscape. With their vivid visual aesthetic and deeply spiritual worldview, they've carved out a uniquely compelling presence in a scene that prizes innovation and artistic vision. Their presence on the MAVERICK channel's streaming platform has been a consistent draw for fans seeking something that feels genuinely distinct.

The sixth and final studio edition of *CHAQLA. no Heya* (CHAQLA.'s Room) airs **July 16, 2026 at 7:00 PM JST** across both YouTube and Niconico. Rather than a conventional sit-down interview, this episode finds the band members folding gyoza together while reflecting candidly on the past half-year, their expectations for the latter half of 2026, and where they're heading creatively. It's a clever format—the ritual of preparing food together tends to lower walls, and apparently CHAQLA. is banking on that intimacy to deliver some genuine, unfiltered conversation.

The episode marks more than just a series milestone. According to the announcement, this broadcast represents a decisive turning point for the studio format itself. The second half of the program will be exclusive to MAVERICK channel members, with special bonus content befitting such a landmark installment.

Previous episodes have featured guest appearances and VTR segments with other artists in the MAVERICK orbit, so there's a sense that this finale is meant to feel genuinely substantial rather than perfunctory.

If you've been following *CHAQLA. no Heya* from the beginning, this is essential viewing. And if you're new to CHAQLA., this could be the perfect entry point—their unfiltered personalities, creative process, and vision for what's ahead all on display in one stream.

Head to their official channels for updates: their website at chaqla.com, YouTube channel, and their fan club, 秘密結社攻撃的狂愛倶楽部 (the Secret Society of Aggressive Crazy Love Club), where deeper community engagement awaits.
