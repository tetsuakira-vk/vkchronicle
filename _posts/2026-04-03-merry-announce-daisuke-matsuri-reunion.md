<p>A legendary three-band convergence is happening this July. Merry have announced **"Daisuke Matsuri!!"** (Daisuke Festival), a special one-night-only event at Tokyo's Zepp Haneda on **July 30th, 2026** — timed to coincide with vocalist Gala's birthday and part of Merry's 25th Anniversary celebration, *Many Merry Days*.</p>
<p>What makes this event historic? For the first time ever, the early 2000s Visual Kei "Big Three" — **Merry, MUCC, and Hizame** — will perform together in a dedicated three-man live. While these bands have shared festival bills countless times, this marks their first headline-only collaboration since the golden age of Japanese visual rock.</p>
<p>The lineup includes Merry, MUCC, and three former Hizame members: vocalist Youana, guitarist Kazu, and bassist Shizumi. Gala personally spearheaded the event's conception, determined to make this dream collaboration a reality after more than two decades.</p>
<p>**The Festival's Touches:**</p>
<p>Ticket tier names are a nostalgia trip for longtime fans, named after iconic tracks from the era: "List Cutter," "R-Rated," "Twilight Apology," and "After All, I Am Just A Dog." These details are a love letter to anyone who lived through VK's golden era.</p>
<p>**Ticket Information:**</p>
<p>Doors open 16:00 / Show starts 17:00 / Expected end 20:10 (drinks additional)</p>
<p>**Pricing (¥):**</p>
<p>- 1F Front Standing (List Cutter): ¥12,000</p>
<p>- 1F Rear Assigned (R-Rated): ¥12,000</p>
<p>- 2F VIP (Twilight Apology): ¥15,000 *(includes front row + merch priority)*</p>
<p>- 2F Assigned (After All, I Am Just A Dog): ¥12,000</p>
<p>**Ticket Sales:**</p>
<p>- **Official Presale:** April 3–6 (19:00–23:59 JST) at TicketTown</p>
<p>- **Eplus Preorder:** April 16–22 (with payment April 28–May 1)</p>
<p>- **General Sale:** May 16, 2026 (10:00 JST) via TicketTown, Eplus, Lawson Ticket, and Ticket Pia</p>
<p>Max 4 tickets per person (VIP: 1 per person).</p>
<p>For more details, visit [Merry's official site](https://merryweb.jp/) or follow them on [X](https://x.com/merry_official).</p>
<p>This is a moment VK history buffs cannot miss. If you lived through the 2000s scene—or want to experience what made it legendary—mark your calendar now.</p>
