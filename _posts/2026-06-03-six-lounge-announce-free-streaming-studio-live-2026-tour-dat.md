SIX LOUNGE is giving fans a chance to catch them live from the comfort of their homes this week. The Tokyo-based outfit has announced a free streaming studio session happening June 4th at 8:00 PM JST via YouTube Live, featuring their latest single "Better than goodbye" (stylized as "さよならじゃない方がいい").

For those unfamiliar, SIX LOUNGE brings theatrical energy and rock sensibility to the modern Japanese music scene. The band has been making waves with their eclectic sound and visual presentation, and this studio session promises an intimate, complete live broadcast straight from a Tokyo recording facility.

The "Better than goodbye" single drops June 3rd across all platforms. The limited edition physical release (¥1,800 / ~$12 USD) packs serious value—you get the full track, a TV-size version, instrumental, vocal-only, and stripped-down versions minus guitar, drums, and bass respectively. Pre-orders are live now through the usual Japanese retailers, each offering exclusive bonuses like special photos, picks, and artwork.

![SIX LOUNGE album artwork](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F02%2F13152637%2FMFS_%25E5%2588%259D%25E5%259B%259E%25E7%2594%259F%25E7%2594%25A3%25E9%2599%2590%25E5%25AE%259A%25E7%259B%25A4JK%25E5%2586%2599s.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

Beyond this week's stream, SIX LOUNGE has plenty on the horizon. Their album "My Favorite Song" arrives March 18th, 2026, offering ten fresh tracks including standouts like "Lion Dances," "Momoko," and "Asia's King." They're also expanding their reach with a regional Northeast Japan tour dubbed "Summer Night in Tohoku" running late July through early August, hitting venues in Miyagi, Iwate, Aomori, Akita, Yamagata, and Fukushima.

The band's recent work has also caught anime attention—their music features in the ongoing TV anime "The Strongest King, Second Life: What Now?" currently airing across multiple Japanese networks and streaming platforms.

Want to catch the free stream? Head to **YouTube Live on June 4th at 8:00 PM JST**. For pre-orders, album details, and tour information, visit SIX LOUNGE's official website, X, Instagram, and YouTube channel—all links available through their main site.
