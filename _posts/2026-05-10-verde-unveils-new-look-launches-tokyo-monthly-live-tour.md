Verde/ is hitting reset with a bold visual refresh and an ambitious new live series that promises to shake up Tokyo's underground scene this summer and fall.

The solo project of Alice Nine vocalist Shou, Verde/ has spent three years building a reputation for sophisticated melodies wrapped in dramatic rock arrangements. Now, with a fresh aesthetic in tow, the band is ready to make some noise with the *NEO TOKYO TRIBE®* monthly gig series—an exclusive Tokyo-only venture that kicks off in August.

Here's what makes this tour unmissable: each show will feature rotating guest vocalists drawn from Shou's inner circle of trusted musicians—his "TRIBE." While the specific guests remain under wraps for now, the promise of unexpected chemistry and collaborative energy should keep fans speculating. This isn't just Verde/ playing the same setlist four times; it's a curated experience that changes with each date.

The tour hits four Tokyo venues across August and October:

- **August 8** – Shibuya Chelsea Hotel
- **September 18** – Shibuya Kinmirai Kaikan
- **September 25** – Hatsudai Doors
- **October 24** – Shibuya Rex

Tickets run ¥6,600 per show (drinks extra), with FC fan club pre-sales starting May 9. Attend all four dates, and you'll earn an exclusive limited edition "Original Live Pass" signed by the band.

Before the tour launches, Verde/ is dropping the new single *Static Eden/* on **June 3, 2026**. A release event and signing happen June 6 at Ultra Shibuya, with a special one-man show featuring the first free set on June 5 at Shimokitazawa Shangri-La.

With support from Matenrou Opera members 優介 (guitar) and 燿 (bass), plus drummer KEN'ICHI, Verde/ continues to prove why Shou's solo venture deserves serious attention.
