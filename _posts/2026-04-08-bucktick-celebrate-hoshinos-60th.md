<p>BUCK∞TICK are pulling out all the stops to celebrate a major milestone. The legendary Japanese Visual Kei pioneers have announced **Ruby Horizon 60**, a one-man live performance dedicated to vocalist and multi-instrumentalist Hoshino Hidehiko's 60th birthday, taking place on **June 16, 2026** at SGC HALL ARIAKE in Tokyo.</p>
<p>This marks BUCK∞TICK's first headlining show of 2026, making it a particularly significant event for the band's dedicated fanbase. Tickets are priced at ¥11,000 (tax included) and will include an exclusive venue gift distributed after the show. Fan club members who maintain "W-member" status (registered with both the official fan club FISH TANK *and* the LOVE & MEDIA PORTABLE mobile site) get early access starting April 8, 2026, while general ticket sales open May 30.</p>
<p>**About BUCK∞TICK:**</p>
<p>Formed in the late 1980s, BUCK∞TICK stands as one of Visual Kei's most influential acts, alongside contemporaries like X Japan and LUNA SEA. Known for their gothic aesthetic, experimental soundscapes, and Hoshino's virtuosic keyboard work, the band has maintained artistic relevance across four decades. Their influence on the Visual Kei movement and Japanese rock cannot be overstated.</p>
<p>**Vinyl Reissues Arrive**</p>
<p>To complement the birthday celebration, BUCK∞TICK is releasing three classic albums on vinyl with newly mastered audio and original artwork:</p>
<p>- **狂った太陽** (*Crazy Sun*, 1991) — February 21 as a 2LP clear vinyl (¥7,150)</p>
<p>- **殺シノ調べ This is NOT Greatest Hits** (1992) — March 21 as a 3LP colored vinyl (¥12,100)</p>
<p>- **darker than darkness -style93-** (1993) — June 23 as a 2LP colored vinyl (¥7,150)</p>
<p>All pressings are 180g limited editions with restored poster inserts — perfect for collectors and longtime fans wanting to experience these classics in pristine condition.</p>
<p>**Don't Sleep on This.** If you've been sleeping on BUCK∞TICK or want to catch them live in 2026, now's the time to act. Check the official site at **buck-tick.com** for full ticket details and pre-orders for the vinyl reissues.</p>
