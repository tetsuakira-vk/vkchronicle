<p>Verde/ is ramping up the intensity with a new single and an ambitious spring tour. The band has unveiled fresh visuals for **"Pulse/"**, set to drop April 1st, 2026—a track that pushes the heavy, loud guitar-driven sound they introduced on last year's *OBSIDIAN/* EP even further into cyberpunk and digital dystopia territory. This is Verde/ at their most aggressive.</p>
<p>**What to expect from "Pulse/":**</p>
<p>The single comes in three formats—luxury edition (¥4,950), live venue edition (¥2,200), and standard CD (¥1,980)—each packed with different bonuses. The standard edition ships with random photo cards and exclusive goodies depending on where you buy it. Tower Records Shinjuku gets a clear file; HMV adds stickers; ULTRA SHIBUYA offers posters.</p>
<p>**The Tour Machine:**</p>
<p>To support the release, Verde/ is launching the **SPRING CIRCUIT: PULSE / STATIC EDEN** tour with a star-studded supporting lineup: guitarist Yusuke and bassist Hikari from *Matenrou Opera*, plus drummer KEN'ICHI. The tour finale on April 24th at BLAZE GOTANDA gets a special upgrade—keyboardist KAZAMI from *DaizyStripper* joins for an exclusive arrangement.</p>
<p>Tour dates hit major cities from early April through late April across six venues. All-standing tickets are ¥6,600 plus drinks. Special merchandise cards unique to each venue will be available only at tour locations.</p>
<p>**What's Next:**</p>
<p>A listening party kicks off March 28th in Shinjuku with guitarist Yusuke in attendance. Verde/ will also appear at an in-store event April 1st at HMV Shibuya. Beyond that, a collab one-man show with vocalist Shou and keyboardist [kei] is scheduled for July 5th at Tokyo Kinema Club, featuring an all-star guest musician lineup and exclusive merch.</p>
<p>If you've been sleeping on Verde/, now's the time to jump in. "Pulse/" sounds like a statement—catch them live if you can.</p>
<p>**Grab tickets and follow Verde/ on** [Twitter](https://twitter.com/Verde__official), [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/verde__official), and [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@ROOM_EVERGREEN).</p>
