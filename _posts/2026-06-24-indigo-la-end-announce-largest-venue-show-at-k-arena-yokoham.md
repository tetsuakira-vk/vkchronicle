indigo la End is stepping into a major milestone with the announcement of their biggest live show to date. The Tokyo-based band unveiled plans for "Aoki Hanataba vol.4" at K Arena Yokohama on February 23, 2027—a landmark performance marking 17 years since their formation and 15 years since their indie debut.

The announcement came during a livestream celebration on June 24, coinciding with the release of their ninth full-length album, *Michita Murasaki* (Filled Purple). For Western audiences less familiar with indigo la End, they're a cornerstone of Japan's contemporary rock scene, known for their atmospheric soundscapes and poetic lyricism that blur the lines between alternative rock and art-rock territories.

![indigo la End promotional image](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F01%2F31195117%2Fmain_artistphoto_small.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

*Michita Murasaki* packages the band's 16-year career into 11 tracks, including advance singles "Kagura," "Whirlpool," and "Heat Haze." Rather than favoring either the "blue" or "red" aesthetic typical of their imagery, the album inhabits a purple gradient—a thematic middle ground reflecting its lyrical complexity. The production features acclaimed art direction from Ryo Kawashima and cover model Ayane Kojima.

The label will release a limited vinyl edition on September 23, 2026, following the CD formats (complete production limited edition with exclusive pouch and scarf, live Blu-ray/DVD editions from their 2026 Nippon Budokan shows, and standard CD).

![K Arena Yokohama show announcement](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F06%2F24194535%2F%25E8%2592%25BC%25E3%2581%258D%25E8%258A%25B1%25E6%259D%259F-vol.4-730x1024.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

**Show Details:**
- **Date:** February 23, 2027 (Tuesday, National Holiday)
- **Venue:** K Arena Yokohama
- **Doors:** 3:30 PM / Start: 5:00 PM
- **Ticket Price:** ¥9,900 (all reserved seating)

Ticket pre-sales begin now through the band's official channels. Mobile member priority access runs through June 28, with general pre-sales extending to July 5. For ticket links and further information, visit the band's official website and secure your spot for what promises to be an essential moment in their career.
