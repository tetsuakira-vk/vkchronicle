<p>Symphonic metal virtuoso KAMIJO is bringing his operatic prowess across Europe this September and October with the *SANCTUARY OF EUROPE* tour—and Western fans have eight major cities to choose from.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar, KAMIJO is the classically trained vocalist and frontman behind the legendary Visual Kei band Versailles, known for blending ornate orchestral arrangements with rock intensity and theatrical grandeur. His solo work maintains that same commitment to baroque-influenced metal and gothic romance that made Versailles essential listening for VK fans worldwide.</p>
<p>**Tour dates are as follows:**</p>
<p>- **September 23** – Helsinki</p>
<p>- **September 24** – London</p>
<p>- **September 26** – Madrid</p>
<p>- **September 28** – Paris</p>
<p>- **September 29** – Cologne</p>
<p>- **September 30** – Berlin</p>
<p>- **October 2** – Warsaw</p>
<p>- **October 3** – Budapest</p>
<p>This is a genuinely impressive routing across Northern, Western, and Central Europe—proof that KAMIJO's fanbase extends far beyond Japan. Whether you've followed him through his Versailles days or discovered his solo material, this tour represents a rare opportunity to witness his powerful vocal delivery and stagecraft live in Europe.</p>
<p>**Ticket and pricing information will be revealed on April 27th at 12:00 CET**, so mark your calendars if you're planning to attend. Given KAMIJO's reputation for visually stunning live performances—expect elaborate costumes, dramatic lighting, and a full orchestral presence—these shows are likely to sell steadily once details drop.</p>
<p>If you're in any of these cities and have been sleeping on KAMIJO's solo catalog, now's the time to catch up. Start with albums like *Shining Diva* or *Schwarzweiß* to get a sense of what's coming. This is symphonic Visual Kei done at the highest level.</p>
