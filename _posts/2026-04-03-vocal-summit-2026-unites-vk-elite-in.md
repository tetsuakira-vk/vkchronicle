<p>The legendary VOCAL SUMMIT returns for its ninth edition this September, and the 2026 lineup is stacked with some of Visual Kei's most respected and technically gifted vocalists.</p>
<p>Taking place on **September 20th at EX THEATER ROPPONGI in Tokyo**, the annual vocalist-focused event will showcase seven powerhouse singers performing both solo sets and collaborative sessions that you won't see anywhere else. The bill reads like a who's-who of VK talent: **Keiji Shiina (SURFACE)**, **HAZUKI (lynch.)**, **En (Skyscraper Opera)**, **Tatsuro (MUCC)**, **Kosuke Tazawa (Rayflower / fuzzy knot)**, **Takui Nakajima**, and **Raimu (Kizu)**.</p>
<p>This marks a significant return for Shiina, who last appeared at the event several years ago. His inclusion alongside MUCC's Tatsuro—one of the most technically impressive vocalists in the scene—and HAZUKI's atmospheric delivery promises unforgettable performances.</p>
<p>The supporting lineup is equally impressive. Guest guitarists **you (ex-Janne Da Arc, Nicori Light Tours)** and **Nao (Arlequino)** will join a stellar backing band featuring session legends **Kenshi Mishiro (G)**, **Kenji (G)**, **Ju-ken (B)**, **kiyo (Keys)**, and **shuji (Drums)**. NoGoD's **Dancho** returns as MC.</p>
<p>**Doors open at 3:15 PM / Show starts at 4:00 PM**</p>
<p>**Tickets:** ¥9,800 (all reserved seating, drinks extra)</p>
<p>**Official presale:** April 3rd, 8:00 PM – April 12th, 11:59 PM via [L-Tike](https://l-tike.com/vocalsummit/)</p>
<p>Since 2018, VOCAL SUMMIT has established itself as a must-attend event for fans who want to witness the technical prowess and emotional range of Visual Kei's elite vocalists in an intimate, collaborative setting. This year's edition looks to be no exception.</p>
<p>**Grab your tickets during the presale window—this show will sell out fast.**</p>
