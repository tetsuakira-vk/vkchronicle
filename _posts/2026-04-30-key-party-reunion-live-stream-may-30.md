<p>A legendary chapter of Visual Kei history is reopening. KEY PARTY, the iconic 1990s Visual Kei talent agency that shaped the movement's early years, is hosting a reunion special — and it's happening live via TwitCasting Premium on May 30th, 2026.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with KEY PARTY's legacy, the agency was instrumental in developing and promoting some of Visual Kei's most influential acts during the genre's formative decade. The agency dissolved over 20 years ago, making this reunion particularly significant for longtime fans who lived through that era.</p>
<p>The inaugural broadcast will be a special 3-hour livestream starting at 6:00 PM JST, featuring an impressive lineup of reunited members from KEY PARTY's roster bands:</p>
<p>**Featured Cast:**</p>
<p>- THOGO (DEFLOWER vocalist)</p>
<p>- Kei (Eliphas Levi vocalist)</p>
<p>- Shuri (Eliphas Levi guitarist)</p>
<p>- TERUHIKO (NéiL bassist)</p>
<p>- Ray (Aliene Ma'riage bassist)</p>
<p>- TOMO (DEFLOWER guitarist)</p>
<p>The broadcast hints at surprise appearances and phone calls from additional members, so longtime fans should keep their eyes peeled for unexpected reunions.</p>
<p>Tickets for the premium livestream are priced at ¥2,300 (approximately $15–18 USD), and the show will also feature commemorative instant photo sets (cheki) signed by individual members — a cherished collector's item in the VK community.</p>
<p>This is more than just nostalgia; it's a chance to witness the people who helped define Visual Kei's aesthetic and sound during its most experimental period. Whether you discovered these bands in the '90s or you're curious about the roots of the movement, this is an unmissable event.</p>
<p>Grab your ticket on TwitCasting Premium and mark your calendar. KEY PARTY's story isn't over yet.</p>
