<p>PaleNeØ is gearing up for their next chapter with the announcement of their second maxi-single *UNISØN*, arriving May 13, 2026. The release marks an exciting progression for the Visual Kei outfit, building on momentum from their debut offerings.</p>
<p>**What's Inside**</p>
<p>The maxi-single will arrive in two editions—Limited and Regular—with both formats featuring the same three new tracks on CD: "Prologue," "Chocolat Maudit," and "Starlight Voyage," each accompanied by karaoke versions. It's a classic approach that gives fans flexibility in how they engage with the new material.</p>
<p>The Limited Edition steps things up significantly with a DVD component that's genuinely substantial. Beyond the "Starlight Voyage" music video, you're getting live footage from Tokyo SpaceOdd (recorded May 11, 2025) capturing "盲導歌" and "in Gloria Dei"—both tracks that should give you deeper insight into PaleNeØ's live energy. There's also an alternate-ending version of "Raison d'etre" and behind-the-scenes footage for "Starlight Voyage." That's serious fan service.</p>
<p>**Lead Single Preview**</p>
<p>The lead track "Starlight Voyage" already has an official music video circulating on YouTube, and it's worth checking out to get a sense of what PaleNeØ are bringing sonically and aesthetically to this release. The French-influenced song titles hint at the band's theatrical sensibilities—very on-brand for contemporary Visual Kei.</p>
<p>**What We Know About PaleNeØ**</p>
<p>For those new to the band, PaleNeØ operate firmly in modern Visual Kei territory, blending dramatic instrumentation with the movement's signature visual presentation. Their approach feels both respectful of VK tradition and contemporary, which has been earning them attention within the Japanese underground rock scene.</p>
<p>**How to Stay Updated**</p>
<p>Make sure you're following their official Twitter (@PaleNeO_info) for pre-order details and any additional announcements closer to the May release date. Given the DVD content from an upcoming live performance, there's likely more news on the horizon—potentially tour dates or venue specifics.</p>
<p>Mark your calendars. *UNISØN* drops May 13, 2026.</p>
