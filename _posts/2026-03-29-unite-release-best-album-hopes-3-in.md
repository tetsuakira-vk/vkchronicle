<p>ユナイト (UNiTE.) is pulling together their finest moments for fans this summer with the upcoming release of their new best album, **HOPES #3**.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with the band, UNiTE. has been a fixture in Japan's Visual Kei underground since the early 2010s, known for their theatrical sound design, emotionally charged vocals, and the kind of intricate arrangements that reward repeated listens. They've cultivated a dedicated fanbase both domestically and internationally by refusing to compromise their artistic vision—a hallmark of the true VK spirit.</p>
<p>**HOPES #3** arrives on **July 8, 2026**, priced at ¥5,000 (roughly $35 USD). The 13-track compilation features a mix of fan favourites and standout deep cuts, showcasing the band's range across nearly a decade of material:</p>
<p>The tracklist opens with "world wide wish" and includes several fan-beloved anthems like "universe," "silence dreamer," and "BadRequest." Notably, the album also features tracks with distinctly Japanese titles—"美空結び" (Misora Musubi), "ナユタの秘密" (Nayuta's Secret), and "液体の夜" (Liquid Night)—suggesting UNiTE. has always balanced their English-language compositions with culturally rooted songwriting.</p>
<p>The album's title itself, **HOPES #3**, signals this is part of an established series within UNiTE.'s discography, making it an essential checkpoint for longtime supporters tracking the band's evolution.</p>
<p>For Western fans who've struggled to keep up with Japanese Visual Kei releases, best albums like this are goldmines—they're thoughtfully curated snapshots of a band's artistic journey, often with remastered sound and fresh artwork. This is your chance to experience UNiTE.'s catalogue in one cohesive package.</p>
<p>Mark your calendars for July 2026. Pre-orders typically go live several weeks before the official release date through Japanese music retailers and international proxy services. Keep an eye on UNiTE.'s official channels for more details.</p>
