<p>Osaka-based visual rock outfit **umbrella** has confirmed the return of their annual self-curated event **〈Alleyway Searchlight〉** (路地裏サーチライト), taking place **June 23, 2026** at **Shinsaibashi BIG CAT** in Osaka.</p>
<p>Running since 2014, this biennial showcase has become a staple of the Kansai underground rock scene. Returning to the same venue and date as last year's edition, umbrella has assembled a compelling four-band lineup that reflects their commitment to elevating the local Osaka scene.</p>
<p>**The Lineup:**</p>
<p>- **umbrella** (hosts)</p>
<p>- **Merry** (3rd consecutive year appearance)</p>
<p>- **The Aza Aza** — a "song-oriented" unit known for their explosive, visceral live energy</p>
<p>- **Arimura Ryutarou** (solo artist)</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with umbrella, the Osaka quartet operates within the broader Japanese rock sphere with theatrical visual aesthetics and dynamic stage presence. The band has deliberately positioned 〈Alleyway Searchlight〉 as their platform to nurture Kansai's rock infrastructure—a region they feel lacks comparable grassroots events.</p>
<p>In his statement, bassist **春** (Haru) acknowledged the event's importance: *"There aren't many events like this in Osaka, and as a band, we felt obligated to step up. But every time we do this, fans express their gratitude, and we're reminded why it's worth the effort."*</p>
<p>Vocalist **唯** (Yui) added a poetic touch, noting that "rain and umbrellas suit this four-band lineup perfectly"—a characteristically clever reference to the band's name.</p>
<p>**Ticket Details:**</p>
<p>- **Pre-sale (official):** March 14–22, 2026 (12:00–23:59 JST)</p>
<p>- **General sale:** April 5, 2026 (10:00 JST)</p>
<p>- **Price:** ¥6,500 advance / ¥7,000 day-of (plus drink charge)</p>
<p>- **Venue:** Shinsaibashi BIG CAT, Osaka</p>
<p>- **Time:** Open 17:00 / Start 17:30</p>
<p>Tickets available via **eplus.jp/umbrella/**</p>
<p>umbrella are also hosting a **birthday one-man show** for bassist 春 on April 30 at Umeda BANGBOO (¥5,000 advance).</p>
<p>If you're in Japan this summer or planning a Kansai trip, this is an essential underground rock event. Secure your tickets now.</p>
