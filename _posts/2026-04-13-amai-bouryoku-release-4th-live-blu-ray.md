<p>Dark and experimental Visual Kei outfit **Amai Bouryoku** are gearing up to unleash their fourth installment in the "流出盤" (Ryūshutsu-ban / Leaked Footage) live series this summer.</p>
<p>The new Blu-ray, titled **"甘い暴力 流出盤④ 九年目の招待"** (Amai Bouryoku Ryūshutsu-ban④ Kunen-me no Shōtai — roughly *"9th Year Invitation"*), drops **June 3rd, 2026**.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with Amai Bouryoku, the Tokyo-based collective has carved out a distinctly theatrical niche within the VK underground. Known for their visceral live performances, intricate visual presentation, and boundary-pushing sonic approach, they've maintained a devoted cult following through their unconventional release strategy and refusal to adhere to mainstream J-rock conventions. Their aesthetic blends horror-tinged imagery with avant-garde musicianship—the kind of uncompromising artistry that defines the more experimental corners of Visual Kei.</p>
<p>The "Ryūshutsu-ban" (leaked footage) series title itself suggests an intentional aesthetic choice: these releases capture raw, unpolished live moments rather than studio-perfected recordings. It's a deliberate rejection of pristine production in favor of capturing the band's visceral, unpredictable stage presence.</p>
<p>This marks the fourth entry in what appears to be an ongoing documentation of the band's live evolution. The subtitle's reference to a "9th year" suggests this release commemorates a significant milestone in Amai Bouryoku's journey—likely tying to their formation or the inception of this particular live documentation project.</p>
<p>Given the underground nature of the Visual Kei scene and the band's selective distribution approach, Western fans should act quickly once pre-orders become available. The "Ryūshutsu-ban" series has developed a strong reputation among collectors, and physical VK releases—especially Blu-rays from more experimental acts—tend to shift fast in the global fanbase.</p>
<p>**Keep an eye on their [official Twitter](https://x.com/sweetviolencejp) for pre-order details and additional release specifications.**</p>
