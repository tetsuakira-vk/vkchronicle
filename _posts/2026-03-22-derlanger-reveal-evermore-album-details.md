<p>D'ERLANGER is building momentum toward their 19th anniversary, and they're not holding back. The legendary Visual Kei act has just unveiled the full tracklist, cover artwork, and fresh band photos for their 11th studio album *Evermore*, arriving April 22, 2026—the exact date the band reformed nearly two decades ago.</p>
<p>The album drops in two formats. The deluxe CD+DVD edition comes as a premium box set with a photo booklet and exclusive DVD content: unreleased fan club live footage from December 2025, plus three songs from last year's special performance, with bonus material breaking down each band member's individual performance moments. The standard CD edition includes a bonus track, "You're gonna be the one that saves me," absent from the deluxe version.</p>
<p>All artwork across both editions was created by band member Tetsu, continuing D'ERLANGER's tradition of maintaining complete creative control. The 10 album tracks span *Evermore*'s full sonic palette—from "undercover of darkness" and "Kiss My Blood" to the poetic "Roses blooming softly at nite" and the introspective closer "Wandering Star."</p>
<p>**Pre-order bonuses are retailer-exclusive**: Amazon Japan offers megajackets, Rakuten Books gives a clear pouch, Seven Net Shopping provides a multi-shoulder bag, and official support shops will distribute B2-sized announcement posters (venues TBA).</p>
<p>But here's the real news: D'ERLANGER has announced a staggering 2026 tour schedule spanning April through December, hitting 30+ cities across Japan. From Umeda and Shinjuku to regional strongholds like Fukuoka and Sapporo, the band is bringing *Evermore* to fans nationwide. This is the kind of extensive touring commitment that defines a major Visual Kei act's relationship with their fanbase.</p>
<p>For Western fans, this marks another significant release from one of Japan's most enduring theatrical rock institutions. D'ERLANGER's ability to balance gothic atmosphere with accessibility has kept them relevant through countless trends—and *Evermore* promises to be no exception.</p>
<p>**Grab pre-orders now through the links above.** Tour tickets and full venue details are available at their official site. If you're serious about Visual Kei, this one's essential.</p>
