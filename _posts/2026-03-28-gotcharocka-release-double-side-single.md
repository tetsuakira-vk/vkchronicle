<p>GOTCHAROCKA is gearing up to deliver fresh material with a striking double A-side single dropping this May. The Tokyo-based rock outfit will unleash "0 MINUS 0 / Wicked Div.1 - 極悪一課 -" on **May 2, 2026**, bringing two contrasting sonic directions on one release.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with GOTCHAROCKA, the band has built a reputation for heavy, theatrical rock that sits at the intersection of industrial and alternative metal—featuring layered guitar work, dynamic vocal delivery, and a visual presentation that commands attention on stage. They've developed a dedicated following among fans who appreciate rock music with genuine edge and complexity.</p>
<p>**Release Details:**</p>
<p>The single will be packaged as a limited CD (containing both tracks) bundled with a **44-page photobook**, priced at **6,000 yen** (approximately $40 USD). Importantly, this is a **live-limited and web-shop exclusive** release, meaning copies will only be available at their live shows and through their official webshop—once sold out, they're gone for good. No major retail distribution means Western fans will need to act fast through import channels or catch them at upcoming shows.</p>
<p>**Tracklist:**</p>
<p>1. 0 MINUS 0</p>
<p>2. Wicked Div.1 - 極悪一課 - (Wicked Div.1 - Most Evil First Division -)</p>
<p>The second track's title hints at darker, more aggressive territory, while the first offers an intriguing counterpoint. The pairing suggests GOTCHAROCKA is exploring contrasting moods within a single statement.</p>
<p>If you've been sleeping on GOTCHAROCKA, this is your sign to dig in. The band deserves far more recognition in Western rock circles. Mark your calendars for the May 2026 release date, and start tracking their upcoming live dates—this limited pressing won't stick around long. Check their official channels for pre-order details and tour announcements.</p>
