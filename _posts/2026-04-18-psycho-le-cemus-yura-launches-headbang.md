<p>Psycho le Cému drummer YURAサマ has teamed up with ヒィロ (ex-ν[NEU] bassist) to tackle a problem every dedicated bandgyaru knows too well: the neck pain that comes the morning after an intense headbanging session at a Visual Kei live.</p>
<p>The collaboration merges YURAサマ's health-conscious expertise—he's an aerobics instructor alongside his drumming duties—with ヒィロ's beauty industry background as owner and esthetician at DearLily salon. Together, they're launching **NECK REBOOT**, a specialized recovery neck support designed specifically for post-show recovery.</p>
<p>The product features far-infrared and magnetic-infused fabric with targeted recovery material on high-impact areas. It's lightweight, quick-drying, and portable enough to pack for live tours—just slip it on after the show. Simple, practical, and exactly what the community needs.</p>
<p>"We've watched fans stop coming to lives because of physical pain," YURAサマ and ヒィロ explained in a joint statement. "We want bandgyaru to headbang for life. That's why we created this product."</p>
<p>**FUNDING & REWARDS**</p>
<p>A Campfire crowdfunding campaign launches **May 9 at 12:00 PM** (JST) through **June 21 at 11:59 PM**. Beyond the neck support itself, backers unlock premium experiences: shooting behind-the-scenes product photography like a band photoshoot, attending beauty seminars on headbang injury prevention, participating in east-west Japan talk events, and more.</p>
<p>The crown jewel? Access to **"Headbangs: Headbang-Only Night"** on **September 8** at Western Eifukuji JAM in Tokyo. An all-star lineup—featuring vocalists マコト (Kaijin Nijuumenso), ミケ (We Are), guitarists ギル (Angelo) and SARSHI (HERO), bassist Rei (DaizyStripper), and drummer YURAサマ—will perform exclusively headbanging songs. Tickets run ¥5,000, with general sales opening July 12.</p>
<p>Before the event, tune into the **live song-request broadcast on April 26 at 8:00 PM** where YURAサマ and ヒィロ will take headbang track suggestions from fans.</p>
<p>[Back the project](https://camp-fire.jp/projects/930740/view) and support a product made by and for the Visual Kei community.</p>
