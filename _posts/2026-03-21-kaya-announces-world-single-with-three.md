<p>Iconic gothic metal act Kaya is ready to expand their discography with a brand-new single, *WORLD*, arriving in both streaming and physical formats this spring.</p>
<p>For those discovering Kaya for the first time, the band has been a cornerstone of the Visual Kei and gothic metal scene since their formation. Known for their atmospheric, melancholic sound and theatrically dark aesthetic, Kaya has built a devoted international fanbase through emotionally resonant compositions and cinematic production values.</p>
<p>The *WORLD* single will hit streaming platforms on **April 20, 2026**, followed by a physical CD+DVD edition on **May 20, 2026** (priced at ¥2,500). The release features three tracks: the title song "WORLD" (written by Aoi Haru), "Queendom" (composed by Euskyss), and "BLESS" (lyrics by Kaya, music by Hora). This collaborative approach—bringing in external songwriters—suggests an exciting creative direction for the band.</p>
<p>All three songs will receive full music videos, which is always a treat for Kaya fans. Digest versions will premiere on YouTube starting April 20, giving Western fans an early taste of what's coming. The physical edition includes a DVD with all three music videos plus a behind-the-scenes making-of featurette—essential viewing for dedicated supporters.</p>
<p>Kaya's ability to craft atmospheric, emotionally layered compositions has always set them apart in the visual kei landscape. With *WORLD*, they're continuing that tradition while exploring fresh creative territory through their collaboration with different songwriters. The gothic sensibilities, orchestral elements, and Kaya's distinctive vocal delivery will undoubtedly shine across all three tracks.</p>
<p>Whether you've been following Kaya since their early days or are just discovering their work, *WORLD* is a must-add to your spring playlist. Mark your calendars for the streaming release in April, and consider grabbing the physical edition in May to support the band directly. Check out more details [here](https://kaya-rose.bitfan.id/contents/365104) and keep an eye on YouTube for the digest videos dropping April 20th.</p>
