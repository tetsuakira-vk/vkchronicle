<p>The experimental Visual Kei project .cute!! is undergoing a major rebrand, officially changing their name to **おしゃぶり。(Oshaburi.)** as of May 5, 2026. The announcement came with a striking new MV spot, signalling a fresh creative direction for the outfit.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with the group, .cute!! formed as a demo project featuring a powerhouse lineup of experienced musicians from the underground scene. The band brings together vocalist **ちゃんりか (Chanrika)**, formerly of Lynoas, with guitarist **ゼノ (Zeno)**, who has played in BLiND OWL, ANSEEK, and 鯖 (SABA). Completing the rhythm section are bassist **莉櫻 (Rio)** and dual drummers **冥 (Mei)**, fresh from ヤレナイ (YARENAI), and **ヒッチコック (HITCHCOCK)**.</p>
<p>The name change to Oshaburi. marks a deliberate evolution rather than a dissolution—the band's social channels already reflect the update, with their official Twitter (@cute_0fficial) announcing the transition as "5/5始動" (launching May 5th). This suggests they're not just rebranding visually or sonically, but potentially repositioning their entire project identity.</p>
<p>While details on what Oshaburi. will sound like remain scarce, the MV spot hints at a possible shift in aesthetic or thematic focus. Given the calibre of musicians involved—particularly Zeno's track record across multiple respected underground acts—there's considerable anticipation about what direction this rebrand will take.</p>
<p>Band name changes in the Visual Kei underground often signal internal creative realignment or a desire to distance themselves from previous project baggage. Whether Oshaburi. represents a heavier sound, a conceptual reinvention, or simply a fresh start, Western fans should keep close tabs on their social media for further announcements.</p>
<p>**Mark May 5, 2026 on your calendar and follow their official channels for music releases and live dates under the new moniker.**</p>
