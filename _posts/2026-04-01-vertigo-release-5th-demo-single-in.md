<p>Fresh blood is flowing into the Visual Kei scene with Vertigo's upcoming 5th demo single release—and the lineup reads like a who's who of exciting project collaborations.</p>
<p>**Vertigo** will drop their latest demo, *誘月* (Yūgetsu, "Alluring Moon"), on **April 12, 2026**. The three-track single showcases a band built on reunited talent and creative synergy. Vocalist いずみ (Izumi) and bassist やますん (Yamasun) both bring experience from the respected act **Schwarz-weiβ**, while Yamasun also contributed to **Temaneki** (テマネキ). Guitarists やっさん (Yassan) and Gucchang complete the lineup, creating a well-rounded ensemble ready to push darkly theatrical boundaries.</p>
<p>The tracklist is lean but intriguing: the title track *誘月*, plus *Thanatos* (evoking the Greek personification of death) and *Lunatic*. Based on the preview footage already circulating, expect the brooding atmosphere and dramatic dynamics Schwarz-weiβ fans will recognize, filtered through a fresh creative lens.</p>
<p>For Western fans still building their familiarity with Japanese underground rock, Vertigo represents exactly the kind of emerging act worth tracking. Demo releases in the VK sphere are often crucial testing grounds—they establish a band's core sound before potentially escalating to larger label support or touring opportunities. The fact that experienced musicians from respected projects are pooling resources here suggests serious intent.</p>
<p>A full music video for *誘月* is already available on YouTube, so you can get a firsthand sense of their aesthetic and sonic direction. The band is actively building presence on social media (check their Twitter at @Vertigo_info), making them relatively accessible for Western fans wanting to follow their journey in real time.</p>
<p>If you're hunting for the next exciting act in Visual Kei's underground ecosystem, Vertigo's *誘月* is essential listening. Mark your calendars for April 12—and keep an eye on where this project heads next.</p>
