Sadie has officially unveiled the supporting acts for their highly anticipated "THE UNITED KILLERS 2026" festival, and the lineup is nothing short of stellar.

Born from Sadie's comeback, THE UNITED KILLERS transforms what could have been a somber anniversary into a celebration of revival. Since its debut, the event has grown from a one-day showcase into a full-scale festival experience. The inaugural edition featured lynch. and キズ, while last year's two-day format brought DIAURA, Waive, and 甘い暴力 into the fold alongside lynch. This year marks a significant shift: a two-day, two-man format that promises intimate, high-stakes performances.

**Day One** will see **MUCC** take the stage alongside Sadie. Few bands have shaped Japan's visual kei landscape—and rock scene at large—quite like MUCC. Their relentless evolution and sonic intensity have made them essential fixtures in the underground and beyond.

**Day Two** brings **DEZERT**, an unmistakable force whose distinctive presence commands every room they enter. The pairing sets up exactly the kind of raw, unfiltered energy that two-man formats thrive on—no filler, just collision.

These aren't just lineup announcements; they're promises of confrontation. When bands of this caliber perform head-to-head, something electric happens. The shared stage becomes a proving ground, and the compressed setlists force every moment to matter.

**Event Details:**

**September 21, 2026** – Sadie vs. MUCC  
**September 22, 2026** – Sadie vs. DEZERT  
Venue: Yokohama Bay Hall  
Doors: 4:30 PM / Show: 5:30 PM  
Tickets: ¥8,800 (standing, pre-sale)

Band-specific advance ticketing opens May 23 at 10 PM JST through June 2 at 11:59 PM JST via Luchini Ticket. Secure your spot through your favorite band's direct link before general sales begin.
