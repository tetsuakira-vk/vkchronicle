Sick² isn't wasting any time. Fresh off the heels of completing their previous one-man tour, the Tokyo-based Visual Kei outfit has already locked in a September 9th digital release for their new single *Mouth to Mouth*—and they're hitting the road hard to support it.

For those new to Sick², the band channels raw, theatrical energy across five members: vocalist 祭 (Matsuri), guitarist たくま, bassist しょう。, and drummer ジェネ★, working together to craft a sound that sits somewhere between aggressive aesthetics and melodic hooks. They've built a dedicated following through relentless touring and an uncompromising approach to their live presence.

![Sick² new single artwork](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2FS_MtoM-580x820.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

The *Mouth to Mouth* single arrives with an accompanying music video—check it out on their official channels to get a feel for what's coming.

What really catches our attention, though, is the scope of their supporting tour, titled "83 Billion Mouths." Running from September through December 2026, Sick² will hit major cities across Japan including Tokyo, Osaka, Nagoya, and beyond. The tour kicks off September 13th at Ikebukuro Black Hole and culminates December 2nd at Shinjuku LOFT with a special final event, *"It's Just Easy Work Deceiving Girls. Vol.19."*

![Sick² band lineup](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F%25E3%2582%25B8%25E3%2582%25A7%25E3%2583%258D%25E2%2598%2585-1-580x869.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

General admission runs ¥4,000 (drinks extra), with pre-sale windows opening in staggered blocks throughout July and August. Those interested in catching Sick² live should head directly to their official tour booking page at eplus.jp for full scheduling and ticketing details.

In the meantime, the band has other dates lined up through August, including appearances at venues like Sugamo Shishio and collaborative shows. Follow their official website and social accounts to stay in the loop on final lineup details and any surprise announcements.
