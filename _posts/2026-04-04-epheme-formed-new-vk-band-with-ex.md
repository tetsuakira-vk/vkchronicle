<p>A promising new Visual Kei band has officially launched. EPHEME (エフェメ), which translates to "ephemeral," formed back in March and is already building momentum with scheduled live appearances across Tokyo's underground scene.</p>
<p>The band's debut live took place on March 24th, marking an exciting entry point for a supergroup lineup drawing from several respected VK acts. EPHEME's next performance will be part of the **cultivate common values** live series on **April 6th at Ikebukuro EDGE** — a perfect opportunity for Western fans in Japan or those traveling to catch them early.</p>
<p>**Meet the members:**</p>
<p>**Emiru** (Vocals) previously fronted CRICK×CLACK (クリッククラック), bringing vocal experience to EPHEME's sound. On **guitar**, **Nazora** (なぞら) has played in both **re:gletA** and **KIMIGAWARUI**, two bands known for intricate instrumentation and darker aesthetics. **Shiu** (志憂) handles **bass duties** and comes from HUMAN ERROR, rounding out a lineup of musicians with serious underground credibility.</p>
<p>The "ephemeral" concept behind their name is intriguing — it suggests a band willing to embrace transience and transformation, which often translates to bold creative choices in Visual Kei. With members drawn from different projects, EPHEME could carve out a unique sonic identity by blending their varied influences.</p>
<p>For Western fans who follow VK's underground circuit closely, EPHEME represents exactly the kind of grassroots energy that keeps the genre vital. New bands with ex-members from respected acts often attract strong word-of-mouth support, and their booking at established venues like Ikebukuro EDGE suggests they're already gaining traction.</p>
<p>**Want to support them?** Check vk.gy for updated tour dates and merchandise information. If you're in Tokyo during their April 6th show, this is a golden opportunity to witness a fresh VK project before they potentially blow up. Follow their social channels for studio recordings — we're eager to hear what Emiru, Nazora, and Shiu have created together.</p>
