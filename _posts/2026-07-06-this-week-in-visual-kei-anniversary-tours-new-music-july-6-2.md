What a week for Visual Kei. We've got bands celebrating milestones, fresh releases dropping left and right, and the live circuit absolutely heating up. If you needed a reminder that VK is thriving right now, this is it.

Let's start with the anniversary celebrations, because there's a lot to unpack. Verde/ is bringing the heat with their 3rd Anniversary Tour "Pale Black/BLACK DAWN," while The Benjamin are gearing up for their 12th Anniversary run with "BECOME THE BORDER." DEZERT, meanwhile, marked 15 years with striking new artist photos and a Hall Tour that's already generating serious buzz. These aren't quiet milestones—they're statements.

On the release front, it's been a packed seven days. ASAGI dropped "NAKED HEART IGNITION" featuring HIROKI, while DOG inThePWO unleashed their 6th album BOWWOW on digital platforms. muque came through with the "HAPPY GROOVY" MV and announced a 2026 tour to boot. the god and death stars also launched a reinterpreted EP with their new lineup, proving that change doesn't mean compromise. And SHAZNA? They're doubling down with both a new 6-Month Digital Single Series kicking off with "Love Banquet" AND a VERSUS 2-Man Live Series launching in November. That's ambition.

Speaking of live shows, the tour calendar is wild right now. KAMIJO is going international with a New York show and Europe tour lined up (plus special guest details still to come). The Benjamin, SHAZNA, and muque all have major tour announcements happening. 04 Limited Sazabys made waves hosting their first-ever gender-separated fan club shows, a move that sparked plenty of conversation. And Fatüm's September Showcase? It sold out VIP tickets in hours. Hours.

What really strikes us this week is the diversity of what's happening. You've got experimental new lineups, international expansion, genre-bending collabs, and deep fan engagement happening simultaneously. Visual Kei isn't just surviving—it's evolving and reaching.

Keep your eyes peeled for those KAMIJO Europe tour details and what muque's 2026 run will look like when dates drop.
