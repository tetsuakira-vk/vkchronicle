<p>DaizyStripper's bassist and vocalist 直(Nao) is stepping into the spotlight with a bold solo venture. His new mini-album *Tessera* arrives on April 12, 2026, marking an exciting expansion of his creative vision beyond the band's signature sound.</p>
<p>**Who is Nao?**</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar, DaizyStripper is a Visual Kei powerhouse known for their theatrical intensity, layered guitar work, and Nao's commanding vocal presence. This mini-album represents a chance for fans to experience his artistic direction on a more personal level—and it's shaping up to be something special.</p>
<p>**What to Expect**</p>
<p>The *Tessera* tracklist reveals an ambitious six-song collection that spans multiple moods. Opening with "夜の湾岸線" (Night Bayshore Line), the album flows through introspective territory with tracks like "変ホ長調で魂を貫通" (Piercing the Soul in E-Flat Major) and "エキゾチックな「哀愁」と「激昂」と「  」" (Exotic "Melancholy" and "Fury" and "  "), before shifting gears into the catchier "Coffee Candy" and energetic "Realize!!!". A stripped-down acoustic version of the opening track appears on the CD-only edition, offering an intimate counterpoint to the full arrangements.</p>
<p>**Star-Studded Collaboration**</p>
<p>What really catches the eye is the guest lineup. Rei (DaizyStripper) contributes bass to the opening track, while 莎奈(Sana) from ユナイト(UNiTE.) handles drums and programming on "Coffee Candy." The standout collab might be "エキゾチックな「哀愁」と「激昂」と「  」," featuring 風弥(Kazami) on piano and 響(Hibiki) from Matenrou Opera on drums—a genuinely intriguing cross-scene partnership.</p>
<p>Nao and collaborator Saya serve as Sound Directors, cementing this as a thoughtfully curated project rather than a quick cash-in.</p>
<p>**Mark Your Calendar**</p>
<p>April 12, 2026 isn't far away. If you've ever been curious about what Nao brings to DaizyStripper, or if you're a completist collector of Visual Kei solo work, *Tessera* deserves your attention. Follow his official Twitter (@_nao_official_) for updates as the release date approaches.</p>
