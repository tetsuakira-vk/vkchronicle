<p>A fresh wave is rising in the Visual Kei underground: SCVMS are officially stepping into the spotlight with their first digital single, "NEW ERA," arriving March 30, 2026.</p>
<p>Little-known outside dedicated VK circles until now, SCVMS represent the kind of raw, hungry energy that keeps the scene alive. The four-piece lineup features vocalist Ruka on lead vocals, guitarist Sho, bassist Haruki, and drummer tsubasa—a tight ensemble built for immediate impact. While details about their formation and creative direction remain mysterious, the band's move into the digital release space signals serious ambitions.</p>
<p>The "NEW ERA" single packs two tracks: the title song "NEW ERA" and "噛跡ノ在処" (literally "the whereabouts of bite marks")—an intriguingly dark title that hints at the thematic darkness many Visual Kei fans crave. With a combined runtime of just over 8 minutes, this is a lean, focused introduction designed to make every second count.</p>
<p>For Western fans still hunting for the next compelling VK discovery, SCVMS arrives at an interesting moment. The scene's established giants continue to evolve, but emerging acts like this remind us why Visual Kei's underground remains creatively vital. Whether SCVMS will lean toward the melodic side, raw aggression, or theatrical presentation remains to be seen—but the aesthetic choices evident in their promotional artwork suggest they understand the theatrical DNA that defines the movement.</p>
<p>The single will be distributed through TuneCore Japan, making it readily available across major streaming platforms worldwide. If you've been craving something fresh from the VK underground, "NEW ERA" is worth your attention when it drops at the end of March.</p>
<p>**Keep up with SCVMS on their [official Twitter](https://x.com/scvms_official) for updates, and mark your calendar for March 30th.**</p>
