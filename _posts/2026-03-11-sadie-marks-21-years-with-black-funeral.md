<p>After two decades of pushing the boundaries of Visual Kei, Sadie is ready to turn the page—and they're doing it in style.</p>
<p>The legendary band, who've spent the last year touring in celebration of their 20th anniversary, will commemorate their 21st year as a group with a special live event titled "BLACK FUNERAL" at Zepp Shinjuku on March 18, 2026. Fresh off the heels of their 20th anniversary tour finale in Nagoya this weekend, Sadie is hitting the ground running with what promises to be another unmissable night.</p>
<p>**About Sadie**</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with the outfit, Sadie has been a fixture in the Visual Kei scene since their formation, building a devoted fanbase with their theatrical performances and distinctive musical approach. The band's longevity speaks volumes in a genre known for lineup changes and disbandments—reaching a 21st anniversary is a genuine milestone worth celebrating.</p>
<p>The newly released visual for this anniversary show maintains Sadie's signature aesthetic, and the "BLACK FUNERAL" title hints at the darker, more dramatic energy fans can expect from the performance.</p>
<p>**Event Details**</p>
<p>**Sadie 21st Anniversary Live "BLACK FUNERAL"**  </p>
<p>**Date:** Wednesday, March 18, 2026  </p>
<p>**Venue:** Zepp Shinjuku  </p>
<p>**Doors:** 5:45 PM / Start: 6:30 PM  </p>
<p>**Tickets:** Available via [Lawson Ticket](https://l-tike.com/concert/mevent/?mid=695005)</p>
<p>If you've been following Sadie's journey or are curious about one of Visual Kei's most enduring acts, this is the perfect opportunity to witness them celebrate two decades-plus of artistry. Mark your calendars and grab your tickets—this one won't last long.</p>
