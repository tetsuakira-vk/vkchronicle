OddRe: has unveiled the music video for their latest digital single "Nemuru Kimi" (Sleeping You), a haunting new track that captures the quiet resonance of nocturnal moments. The three-piece band continues to establish themselves as one of Japan's most compelling emerging visual rock acts with this introspective new release.

The song begins from the concept of "traveling through radio waves to meet someone," distilling the subtle atmospheres and lingering echoes that inhabit the silence of night. Directed by Nanase Akiyama, the accompanying MV transforms this intimate premise into visual poetry—three band members occupy a single room, their presence anchoring fragmented memories that drift through darkness. Bathed in muted, cool tones, the video masterfully interweaves stillness and motion, punctuating the visual narrative with brief performance sequences that embody the song's undulating tension and raw impulse. The result feels like watching emotion crystallize in real time.

"Nemuru Kimi" has already made significant radio impact across Japan, securing power play and recommendation slots on 46 stations nationwide, with the track receiving consistent airplay throughout May 2026.

**Tour Announcement**

OddRe: will launch their first-ever headline tour, **OddRe: 1st ONEMAN TOUR "CODA,"** across three major Japanese cities in July 2026:

- **July 1** — Nagoya Club Quattro
- **July 2** — Umeda Club Quattro (Osaka)
- **July 8** — Shibuya Club Quattro (Tokyo)

All shows open at 18:00, with performances starting at 19:00.
