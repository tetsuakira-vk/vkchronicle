<p>After 14 years away from European stages, Kaya is making a triumphant return—and he's bringing Schwarz Stein along for the ride.</p>
<p>The gothic lolita pioneer and his unit have officially announced a European tour slated for August and September 2026. For Kaya, this marks a significant milestone: his first continent-wide tour since 2012. For Schwarz Stein, the journey across Europe represents another exciting chapter in their ongoing collaboration.</p>
<p>**Who is Kaya?**</p>
<p>If you're new to the Visual Kei underground, Kaya is a legendary figure in the gothic lolita and EBM-influenced VK scene. Known for his striking feminine aesthetic, ethereal vocals, and darkly romantic compositions, he's been a cornerstone of Japan's alternative music landscape since the 1990s. His solo work and various projects—including Schwarz Stein—blend gothic atmospherics, electronic elements, and theatrical presentation into something wholly unique within Visual Kei.</p>
<p>Schwarz Stein, his unit with collaborators, has become increasingly active in recent years, bringing Kaya's signature sound to new audiences while maintaining the avant-garde edge that defines his artistic vision.</p>
<p>**What to expect**</p>
<p>While specific dates, venues, and ticket information are still being finalized, expect this tour to be a comprehensive European run spanning major cities. Given Kaya's dedicated fanbase across the continent and the growing interest in Visual Kei among Western audiences, this is genuinely unmissable for anyone into gothic and experimental Japanese rock.</p>
<p>The official announcement came via Kaya's website, with additional details to follow soon. Keep your eyes on his social media and ticket platforms for the full itinerary.</p>
<p>**Mark your calendars** for summer 2026—a Kaya & Schwarz Stein European tour is the kind of VK event that doesn't happen often. If you've been waiting for the chance to experience his theatrical grandeur live, this is it. More details coming soon.</p>
