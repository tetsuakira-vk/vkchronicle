<p>Digital singles have become increasingly important for Visual Kei bands looking to maintain momentum between full releases, and "ワールドエンド" positions Tenebrae as part of a broader wave of VK acts leveraging streaming platforms to reach listeners worldwide. Whether this is a standalone release or part of a larger project remains to be seen, but the April 27 date gives fans something concrete to mark on their calendars.</p>
