04 Limited Sazabys is doing something unprecedented: splitting a single-day fan club concert into two gender-exclusive performances. The Nagoya-based visual rock outfit will celebrate the eighth anniversary of their fan club "YON TOWN" with a dual-format show on August 8, 2026, at COMTEC PORTBASE in Nagoya—and the band's already building anticipation around what each setlist might reveal.

![04 Limited Sazabys YON TOWN 8th Anniversary visual](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F07%2F03195207%2F04LS_yontown8th.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

For those unfamiliar with the group, 04 Limited Sazabys have earned their place as one of Japan's most theatrical and genre-defying rock acts, blending visual kei aesthetics with heavy instrumentation and emotional depth. Their fan community is notoriously dedicated, making this milestone event a major moment for longtime supporters.

The afternoon matinée—dubbed "Midsummer Town Citizen Female Festival"—runs at 2:30 PM and 3:30 PM and is exclusively for female attendees. The evening show, "Midsummer Town Citizen Male Festival," takes over at 6:00 PM and 7:00 PM for male fans only. YON TOWN members began early ticket applications on July 3, 2026.

Beyond this experiment, 04 Limited Sazabys also confirmed their ONE MAN TOUR 2026 schedule, hitting major venues across Japan through October. Dates include stops at KT Zepp Yokohama (October 2), Zepp Fukuoka (October 8), Zepp Osaka Bayside (October 9), and multiple nights at Zepp Haneda (October 21–22 and beyond).

For complete details on the fan club show and full tour information, head straight to the band's official website and check out their ticketing page. YON TOWN members should act fast—fan club-exclusive events like this tend to fill quickly.
