Nothing's Carved In Stone has unveiled the full live performance video of "Black Train," a standout track from their latest album *Fire Inside Us*. The footage captures the band delivering the song during the final show of their *Fire Inside Us Tour* at Zepp DiverCity Tokyo—a venue that's become synonymous with major Japanese rock performances.

For those unfamiliar with NCIS, the Tokyo-based outfit has carved out a reputation for intricate, emotionally charged rock music that balances technical precision with theatrical presence. *Fire Inside Us* showcases the band at a creative peak, and "Black Train" exemplifies why they've built such dedicated Western followings in recent years.

![Nothing's Carved In Stone performing at Zepp DiverCity](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F05%2F30033404%2F01_36387-3-1-1024x768.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

The video originally aired as a segment on TV Tokyo's *Choonpa* program, with the full broadcast available via TVer starting May 30th at 12:00 PM JST. If you missed the television airing, this is your chance to experience the band's Zepp DiverCity finale in complete form.

Looking ahead, Nothing's Carved In Stone are gearing up for an exciting touring cycle. Their *Hand In Hand Tour 2026* pairs them with notable collaborators across three major Japanese venues:

**Tour Dates:**
- September 30 (Wed) — KT Zepp Yokohama, Kanagawa w/ [Alexandros]
- October 2 (Fri) — Nagoya Diamond Hall, Aichi w/ TBA
- October 30 (Fri) — Gorilla Hall Osaka w/ TBA

Doors open at 6:00 PM; performances begin at 7:00 PM. General standing tickets are priced at ¥6,300, with student discounts available at ¥4,000.

The pairing with [Alexandros] alone signals this will be a memorable night for Japanese rock fans. If you're planning to catch these shows, get your tickets sorted early—lineup announcements for the remaining dates should drop soon.
