Ryugu-Jo is gearing up for a summer release with their new EP *MOTTO*, arriving in both physical and digital formats. The theatrical act has announced a staggered rollout that gives fans multiple ways to experience the new material—and teases a standout lead single dropping imminently.

The EP arrives digitally on July 15, 2026, with the CD following a week later on July 22. But before that, the band is sharing the single "Hoero!!!" on May 27, and it's already available for pre-save across streaming platforms. According to the announcement, "Hoero!!!" showcases Ryugu-Jo's distinctive ability to blend unconventional instrumentation—in this case, upright bass—with deeply melodic, enka-influenced songwriting. The track is framed as an anthem for anyone pushing through a difficult time, offering encouragement when it's needed most.

![Ryugu-Jo "Hoero!!!" single artwork](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F05%2F26121201%2F%25E3%2581%25BB%25E3%2581%2888%25E3%2582%258D-%25E9%25BE%258D%25E5%25AE%25AE%25E5%259F%258E%25E3%2583%25A1%25E3%2582%25A4%25E3%2583%25B3A%25E5%2586%2599.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

For collectors, the CD comes in two editions. The limited production version (¥15,000) is a premium package bundling two Blu-rays containing footage from Ryugu-Jo's March 1 arena show at TOYOTA ARENA TOKYO, plus behind-the-scenes content. The standard edition (¥2,500) offers the CD alone in jewel case format, with unspecified bonus materials for first-run copies.

![Limited edition packaging](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fnew-img.barks.jp%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2026%2F05%2F26121412%2F%25E5%25AE%258C%25E5%2585%25A8%25E7%2594%259F%25E7%2594%25A3%25E9%2599%2590%25E5%25AE%259A%25E7%259B%25A4jpg.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

Pre-orders opened immediately, with retailer-exclusive bonuses ranging from B2 posters and signed bromides to acrylic keychains and clear files—perfect for dedicated fans looking to complete their collection. Sony Music Shop is also offering a special bundle deal: buy multiple copies during early pre-order and receive a seven-photo set.

The band's momentum doesn't stop after the summer release either. Ryugu-Jo has already booked a two-day stint at Keio Arena TOKYO on February 20 and 21, 2027, giving fans something substantial to look forward to.

**Catch "Hoero!!!" when it launches May 27, and pre-order *MOTTO* now through the official links. Streaming pre-saves and CD pre-orders are live.**
