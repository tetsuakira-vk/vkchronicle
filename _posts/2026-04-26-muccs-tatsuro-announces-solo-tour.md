<p>MUCC's talented vocalist Tatsuro is taking his solo project on the road this fall with the announcement of a brand-new live house tour. The "Live House Tour [Shinjingan]" (真人間) will kick off in September 2026, marking an exciting next chapter for the musician following his successful "Villains Chapter 2" tour.</p>
<p>The announcement came on April 26th during the final show of his previous tour at Asakusa Fleur Theatre in Tokyo, where a packed audience received the exciting news firsthand. This new venture promises to explore fresh creative directions, with the tour title "Shinjingan" (literally "true human") hinting at a distinct artistic vision.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar, Tatsuro is the creative force behind MUCC, one of Japan's most influential alternative rock acts known for their experimental sound and theatrical live performances. His solo work showcases a different dimension of his artistry, allowing him to explore musical territories beyond the band's scope.</p>
<p>**Tour Details:**</p>
<p>The six-date tour will span from September 12th through October 4th, 2026, covering major cities across Japan:</p>
<p>- **September 12** – Live House J (Nagano)</p>
<p>- **September 13** – Vanvan V4 (Kanazawa)</p>
<p>- **September 23** – Shibuya Music Hall (Tokyo)</p>
<p>- **September 26** – New Yokohama Side Beach (Kanazawa)</p>
<p>- **October 3** – Club Upset (Nagoya)</p>
<p>- **October 4** – Umeda Bangboo (Osaka)</p>
<p>All venues open at 4:15 PM with performances starting at 5:00 PM.</p>
<p>**Ticket Information:**</p>
<p>Fan club pre-sales begin May 18th for Aka no Shou + SWAMP members, with general public tickets going on sale July 18th through e-plus.</p>
<p>If you're a fan of MUCC or interested in exploring Tatsuro's solo artistry, this is your chance to experience something special. Head to his official website for full tour updates and ticket details!</p>
