<p>Lyrical:code is gearing up to unveil their most ambitious project yet. The electronic Visual Kei project helmed by sa-torunn (guitarist of Lilia'luce) will drop their highly anticipated 5th EP, *Under the Moonlight*, on April 26, 2026.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with Lyrical:code, the project fuses synth-driven atmospherics with ethereal vocals and the theatrical sensibilities that define Visual Kei. Sa-torunn has carved out a distinctive sonic space that feels both haunting and hypnotic—perfect for fans of acts like Versailles or experimental VK acts exploring electronic territories.</p>
<p>This new EP showcases an impressive roster of guest vocalists, signaling a collaborative approach that elevates the project's ambition. The seven-track tracklist reads like a nocturnal journey:</p>
<p>**Tracklist:**</p>
<p>1. Starry Night Moonlit Sky feat. KOKOMI</p>
<p>2. Mirror Water Reflection feat. okogeeechann</p>
<p>3. inVoid feat. みそらみみ</p>
<p>4. Heavenly Color Symphony feat. 実稀</p>
<p>5. Pure White Lily feat. 実稀</p>
<p>6. Holy-chant-requiem feat. 実稀</p>
<p>7. SEASONS feat. okogeeechann</p>
<p>The thematic cohesion is palpable—lunar imagery, reflection, and celestial symbolism weave throughout the track titles, suggesting a conceptual narrative that fans will want to unpack. The decision to feature 実稀 across three tracks hints at her importance as a central voice in the EP's emotional core.</p>
<p>A preview was unveiled at the 2026 Spring M3 event, and the response from the VK community has been overwhelmingly positive. You can catch a taste of what's coming via the official [YouTube preview](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eXxBhT4CSeg).</p>
<p>If you've been sleeping on Lyrical:code, *Under the Moonlight* is the perfect entry point. Mark your calendars for April 26—this is essential listening for anyone seeking innovative, atmospheric Visual Kei with genuine emotional depth. Stream the preview now and prepare yourself for what promises to be one of 2026's standout VK releases.</p>
