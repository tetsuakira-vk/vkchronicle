The solo project un autre éden—helmed by 二人静 (Futarishizuka) of Misanthrope—is gearing up to release its second single this spring. Titled "conte de fées" (French for "fairy tale"), the release arrives on May 5, 2026, continuing the project's poetic and introspective aesthetic.

For those unfamiliar with the artist: 二人静 is known for his atmospheric, melancholic approach to Visual Kei, bringing a distinctly theatrical sensibility to Misanthrope's sound. The un autre éden project ("another Eden" in French) represents a deeply personal creative venture, allowing him to explore more experimental and introspective territory outside of his main band commitments.

The new single features two tracks:

1. Reincarnation eve
2. 十三月ノ明晰夢 (The Lucid Dream of the Thirteenth Month)

Both titles hint at the dreamlike, philosophical themes that characterize un autre éden's sonic universe. The pairing of Western and Japanese linguistic elements—a hallmark of the project's identity—continues here, blending European elegance with distinctly Japanese sensibilities. If the project's debut material is any indication, fans can expect lush production, intricate arrangements, and 二人静's distinctive vocal delivery.

This marks an exciting continuation for a project that's been carving out its own niche within the broader Visual Kei landscape. un autre éden occupies a unique space: experimental enough to surprise longtime fans of 二人静's work, yet cohesive enough to feel like a natural creative extension rather than a departure.

The single will be available through standard retail channels and digital platforms on May 5, 2026. For the latest updates and pre-order information, keep an eye on the project's official Twitter account—it remains the most reliable source for announcements and behind-the-scenes glimpses into un autre éden's creative process.
