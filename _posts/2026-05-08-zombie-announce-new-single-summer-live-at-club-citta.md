Visual Kei outfit ZOMBIE have exciting news for fans: a fresh single is on the way, and they're heading to one of Japan's most iconic venues this summer.

The band—fronted by vocalist Kureha alongside guitarists izuna and ZOMBIE, bassist Tēdī, and with vocal support from VOICE—announced the upcoming release via their official Twitter account on February 22, 2025. While full details remain under wraps, the band confirmed that new music is in development and promises that ZOMBIE "will continue to evolve" as artists.

For those unfamiliar with ZOMBIE, the group has steadily carved out a presence in the visual kei underground with a lineup that blends multiple vocal talents and intricate instrumentation. The addition of guest musicians like Toshiki (SPELL BOX) on keyboards and support vocalists demonstrates the band's commitment to expanding their sonic palette.

**Mark Your Calendars**

ZOMBIE will perform at **Club Citta' in Kawasaki on July 8, 2025**—one of Japan's most respected live spaces. Club Citta' has hosted countless iconic Visual Kei acts over the decades, making this an unmissable opportunity to catch the band in an intimate, high-energy setting.

Previous recent activity includes their December 2023 performance with Dr. REIKA, so live momentum is clearly building.

**What's Next?**

Though specific pricing and ticket information isn't yet available through English-language channels, fans should keep an eye on ZOMBIE's social media and vkdb.jp (a comprehensive Visual Kei database) for announcements. Japanese fans can typically purchase tickets through standard domestic channels once they go on sale.

The mysterious nature of the single's release date actually works in ZOMBIE's favor—it's got fans speculating and engaged. Given the band's track record of thoughtful artistry and live presence, this new chapter could be their most ambitious yet.

**If you're into melodic Visual Kei with experimental edge, follow ZOMBIE's social channels now so you don't miss the single drop or ticket sales. Club Citta' shows move fast.**
