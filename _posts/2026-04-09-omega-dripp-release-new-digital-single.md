<p>Japanese Visual Kei act OMEGA DRIPP is gearing up to unleash fresh material this spring with the digital single "SUDARIUM," set to drop on April 10, 2026.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with the band, OMEGA DRIPP brings the theatrical darkness that defines modern Visual Kei—expect dramatic instrumentation, haunting vocals, and the kind of conceptual intensity that keeps the genre alive in Japan's underground rock scene. They've carved out a solid following among Western VK fans who appreciate bands that don't shy away from exploring heavy, atmospheric territory.</p>
<p>The title "SUDARIUM" carries intriguing historical and religious connotations, suggesting this release may continue the band's thematic approach to songwriting. Whether the single marks a shift in their sonic direction or builds on their established sound remains to be seen, but the cryptic nature of the announcement has already sparked speculation among fans.</p>
<p>This digital release is a great entry point if you haven't explored OMEGA DRIPP's catalog yet—singles are often perfect for discovering whether a band's aesthetic and sound align with your tastes. The band has consistently delivered compelling material that sits comfortably alongside other contemporary Visual Kei acts making waves in the Japanese underground.</p>

Digital singles typically become available across major streaming and purchase platforms, so check your usual spots—Bandcamp, iTunes, Spotify, and YouTube Music—when the release date hits.</p>
