The aristocratic vocalist is ramping up his international presence with an exciting slate of announcements. KAMIJO, best known as the frontman of the legendary gothic rock unit Versailles, continues to build momentum as a solo artist with plans spanning both North America and Europe.

For those unfamiliar with KAMIJO's work: beyond his towering presence in Versailles, he's established himself as a compelling solo performer, blending symphonic elements with theatrical gothic sensibilities. His voice—rich, commanding, and deeply expressive—commands attention whether delivering aristocratic narratives or intimate character studies.

The New York show will feature support from two rising names in the visual kei underground: MASASHI and CANARY KASEY. What makes this lineup particularly intriguing is the appearance of Mana, the enigmatic genius behind Malice Mizer and longtime collaborator within the gothic rock sphere. His involvement suggests this won't be a standard live experience—Mana's presence typically signals something conceptually ambitious.

On the touring front, KAMIJO has mapped out a European leg, though specific dates and venues are still being finalized. Given the strength of his fanbase across the UK and continental Europe, these dates are likely to draw serious interest from devoted followers who've followed his career trajectory since Versailles' international breakthrough.

KAMIJO's momentum reflects the broader resurgence of gothic rock and symphonic visual kei aesthetics among Western audiences. His ability to command a stage both visually and vocally—combined with his knack for thoughtful album concepts—has earned him a distinct position within the scene.

For the most current details on ticketing, venue information, and exact tour dates, head directly to KAMIJO's official channels and social media accounts. Given the caliber of the support lineup and Mana's involvement, these shows are shaping up to be unmissable for serious gothic rock enthusiasts.
