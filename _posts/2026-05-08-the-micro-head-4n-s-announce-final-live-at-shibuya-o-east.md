After 15 years of pushing the boundaries of Visual Kei, THE MICRO HEAD 4N'S are ready to close the chapter on one of the scene's most distinctive projects.

The Tokyo-based band has announced their final live performance, scheduled for December 18, 2026, at Shibuya Spotify O-EAST. This show marks the end of an era for a group that has consistently defied genre conventions, blending aggressive electronics with theatrical rock sensibilities.

THE MICRO HEAD 4N'S built their reputation on fearless experimentation and provocative stage presence. Over the past decade and a half, they've released several critically acclaimed albums including *REBIRTH -the 3rd form-* (2019), *Deeper Than Black* (2016), and *REVERBERATIONS* (2013). Their sound—characterized by distorted synths, pulsating rhythms, and raw vocal intensity—has earned them a dedicated following both domestically and internationally.

The band's final tour, titled "HELLO MY CLONE," will document this closing chapter before the December farewell show. Fans who have followed their journey through multiple line-up changes and sonic evolutions will have one last opportunity to witness their uncompromising live energy.

While details about ticket pricing and official sales channels remain limited at present, interested fans should monitor their official website (themicrohead4ns.jp) for updates. The band also announced a March 2026 release that will serve as a bookend to their legacy—further details about this project are expected to be shared in the coming months.

For Western fans of experimental and boundary-pushing Visual Kei, THE MICRO HEAD 4N'S have always represented the genre's willingness to challenge itself. This final show is a chance to pay tribute to 15 years of uncompromising artistry. Keep your eyes on official channels for ticket announcements and don't miss this historic farewell.
