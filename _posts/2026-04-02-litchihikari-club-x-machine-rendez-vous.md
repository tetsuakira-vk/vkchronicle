<p>A forgotten gem from 2012 has finally made its way to streaming platforms. Litchi☆Hikari CLUB and machine's collaborative split mini-album *Rendez-vous* is now available on all major streaming services, bringing this rare release to Western fans who may have missed its original limited run.</p>
<p>For those unfamiliar, Litchi☆Hikari CLUB is a theatrical Visual Kei unit known for their avant-garde aesthetics and experimental sound design, while machine has recently made waves with their 2024 revival featuring new members. This split represents an interesting snapshot of the Japanese underground scene from over a decade ago—a time when physical releases with exclusive DVD bonuses were the standard for limited-edition drops.</p>
<p>The original 2012 release came in three editions: limited type A and type B, each packaged with exclusive music video DVDs ("innocent fever" and "Phantom of the end" respectively), while a standard edition contained identical audio across all versions. Six tracks make up the release: "innocent fever," "Shinjitsu no Dangan" (真実の弾丸 / Truth's Bullet), "Kaze to Tomoni Sarinu" (風と共に去りぬ / Gone with the Wind), "Phantom of the end," "wonder rondo," and "WARNING."</p>
<p>What makes this streaming release particularly exciting is the newly uploaded full-length music video for "Phantom of the end." Previously, both tracks existed only as short teaser versions on YouTube—fans can finally experience the complete visual presentation. The Litchi☆Hikari CLUB video for "innocent fever," however, remains unavailable in full form at the time of writing.</p>
<p>This distribution timing is no accident. Machine's recent streaming push—their revival material and best-of compilation added in February—has clearly paved the way for their collaborative work to reach digital platforms. It's a welcome reminder that deep cuts from Visual Kei's underground scene deserve second lives in the streaming era.</p>
<p>Whether you're a longtime fan rediscovering this collaboration or a newer listener exploring the VK archives, *Rendez-vous* is essential listening. Stream it now through your preferred platform, and don't sleep on that newly available "Phantom of the end" video.</p>
