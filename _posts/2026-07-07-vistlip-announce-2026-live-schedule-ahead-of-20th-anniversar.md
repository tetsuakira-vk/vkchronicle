With their 20th anniversary looming in 2027, vistlip are ramping up the momentum—and their live schedule to match. Fresh off their 19th anniversary celebration at Zepp Diver City on July 7th, the Tokyo visual kei veterans have unveiled an exciting slate of performances spanning the latter half of 2026, signaling the band's determination to make their two-decade milestone unforgettable.

For those unfamiliar with vistlip, the five-piece (vocalist 智, guitarists Yuh and 海, bassist 瑠伊, and drummer Tohya) have carved out a distinctive niche in the visual kei scene with their theatrical energy and genre-blending approach. Their recent album DAWN showcases the band's continued evolution, available now in both CD+DVD and CD-only formats.

![vistlip promotional artwork](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2Fvistlip_%25E3%2582%25A2%25E3%2583%25BC%25E5%2586%2599_%25E7%25B8%25A6%25E3%2582%25B5%25E3%2583%2596-580x826.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

The announced live dates span four distinct events. First up is a **fan club-exclusive show on October 19th** at Shibuya Pleasure Pleasure, charmingly billed as "Bistro Academy Shibuya Branch Light Music Club Culture Festival Stage Preparation Edition"—a delightfully tongue-in-cheek concept that's pure vistlip. Tickets are ¥6,500 advance / ¥7,500 day-of (drinks additional), and fan club members can join the V.I.P. List or Members List presales beginning July 13th and August 1st respectively.

December sees the action intensify with three major events. The band's curated "Party On -Deluxe-" event returns to Kawasaki Club Citta' on December 4th, featuring senior and junior bands in a festive lineup—full details coming soon. Then vistlip closes out the year with back-to-back one-man shows in Osaka (December 20th at Big Cat) and Tokyo (December 23rd at Shibuya Spotify O-East).

These year-end performances promise particular significance. Scheduling solo shows during the busiest season of the Japanese calendar underscores vistlip's commitment to their fanbase and hints at what's to come as they approach their historic milestone.

For ticket information, presale dates, and complete details, head to **vistlip's official website** and check their **official fan club portal**. Don't sleep on these opportunities—the band's momentum heading into their 20th anniversary is impossible to ignore.
