<p>Visual Kei continues to welcome fresh voices, and RE:lNa is building momentum with their highly anticipated second single. The band will release "届かなかったすべての夜に" (All the Nights That Never Reached) on **July 15, 2026**.</p>
<p>RE:lNa represents an intriguing collision of established VK talent, bringing together seasoned musicians from respected acts within the scene. The lineup features vocalist Kei leading the charge, with guitarist 華雪-Kayuki- (formerly of 2nd Dyz) providing melodic firepower alongside Tokkiy from Ru:natic on second guitar. The rhythm section is anchored by bassist Hiromaru。(ex-Gimmick.) and drummer 玲眞-Shima- (formerly of Xirclett), creating a well-rounded ensemble that blends experience with creative vision.</p>
<p>The band's first single generated considerable interest within the Western VK fanbase, and anticipation is building around what direction "届かなかったすべての夜に" will take. The title—which translates to something deeply introspective about unreached nights—suggests themes of longing and melancholy, staples of emotional Visual Kei storytelling.</p>
<p>What makes RE:lNa particularly compelling is how the individual members' previous projects hint at their sonic palette. The combination of musicians with backgrounds in melodic VK acts suggests listeners can expect sophisticated arrangements, layered guitar work, and the kind of theatrical sensibilities that define the genre. Whether the band leans toward the more aggressive side of Visual Kei or embraces darker, more atmospheric territory remains to be seen.</p>
<p>For Western fans keeping tabs on Japan's underground music scene, RE:lNa represents exactly the kind of project worth monitoring—a supergroup of sorts that could easily break through to wider recognition. The July 2026 release date gives you plenty of time to familiarize yourself with their debut material and prepare for what comes next.</p>
<p>**Mark your calendars for July 15, 2026, and follow RE:lNa's official Twitter for updates, teasers, and additional announcements leading up to the single's arrival.**</p>
