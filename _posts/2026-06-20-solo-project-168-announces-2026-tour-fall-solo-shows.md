Ex-彩冷える vocalist 向日葵 is stepping into the spotlight with a major solo push this year, unveiling both a band-format tour and intimate acoustic performances that showcase different facets of his artistry.

For those unfamiliar, 向日葵's work with 彩冷える established him as a compelling voice in Visual Kei's melodic landscape. His solo project 168 (Iroha) now builds on that foundation with carefully curated live experiences designed to connect directly with fans on multiple levels.

The first phase kicks off with the "君に降る雨を、僕は音にする。" (I Turn the Rain Falling on You into Sound) tour, launching late June across three major cities. The concept reflects 向日葵's vision: transforming the melancholy of rainy season into uplifting music that brings smiles to listeners' faces. He's assembled an impressive supporting lineup featuring guitarist Ruiza (Redo/D), bassist NAOKI (ex-Kagrra,/FANTASISTA), drummer YUKI, and manipulator IPPEI (ex-FEEL/絶対的メメント) — musicians whose combined chemistry promises something special.

![168 Project Visual](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F168_WEB-580x410.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

**Tour Dates:**
- **June 27** – Osaka RUIDO
- **June 28** – Nagoya HOLIDAY NEXT NAGOYA
- **July 5** – Tokyo Ikebukuro EDGE (includes after-party)

Tickets run ¥6,500 advance / ¥7,000 door (all standing), available through e+.

Following the tour momentum, autumn brings "VOICE6" — a stripped-back solo concert series where 向日葵 performs with only piano accompaniment. This intimate format emphasizes vocal storytelling and emotional directness. Each VOICE6 event pairs the main concert with a fan meeting featuring talk, signatures, and handshakes.

![VOICE Project Visual](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2FVOICE_WEB-580x821.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

**VOICE6 Dates & Venues:**
- **September 5** – Osaka Muse Box (Concert ¥5,000 / Fan Meeting ¥3,000)
- **November 15** – Tokyo Kagurazaka Tenmado (Concert ¥5,000 / Fan Meeting ¥3,000)

Pre-sales open June 20 via TiGET.

This dual approach—energetic ensemble arrangements alongside vulnerable solo moments—positions 向日葵 as an artist unafraid to show multiple dimensions. Whether you're drawn to the orchestrated production of 168 or the raw vocal authenticity of VOICE6, there's something here worth experiencing.

Follow 向日葵's updates on [X](https://x.com/aoi_official) and [Instagram](https://instagram.com/AOI_OFFICIAL) for the latest details, or secure your tickets through the official links above.
