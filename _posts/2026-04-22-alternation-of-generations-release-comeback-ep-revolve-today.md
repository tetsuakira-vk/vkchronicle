# Alternation of Generations' Highly Anticipated Comeback EP 'REVOLVE' Released Today

Progressive pop band Alternation of Generations' long-awaited comeback EP 'REVOLVE' is released and available for streaming today.

Alternation of Generations was formed in 2022 by DANCHO (Vo), YOSHIHIRO (G), Ryosuke (B), FUMIYA (Dr), and other highly skilled musicians well-known in the rock scene. With an assembly of virtuoso musicians, they've established their own unique genre called "progressive pop"—pairing complex time signatures and dynamic song progressions with catchy melodies—and generated significant buzz.

Breaking their roughly four-year silence, the 2nd EP 'REVOLVE' marks their highly anticipated return. Compared to their previous work, this album is even more intricate while perfecting the pop sensibility, making it a true representation of Alternation of Generations. Whether you love odd time signatures or not, we encourage you to experience its essence at least once.

Today, a full song preview trailer has also been released to commemorate the release of the 2nd EP 'REVOLVE'. The video captures the diverse range of songs that Alternation of Generations has to offer, so be sure to check it out.
