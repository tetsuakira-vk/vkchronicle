<p>A fresh face has emerged in the Visual Kei underground: **KYMM (カイム)**, a newly formed band that brings together an intriguing mix of seasoned musicians and creative talent.</p>
<p>The lineup features **Yumekawa Minmi** on vocals, who previously fronted MELVEL and YUMELEEP—both bands rooted in the VK underground scene. Minmi brings an established vocal presence and performance experience to the group. On guitar, **Shiina Mio** joins from CANZEL and UNiTE., adding another layer of underground credibility; alongside her is **Yuh** from the long-running Visual Kei act **vistlip**, whose extensive background in the scene should add maturity and musicianship to KYMM's sound.</p>
<p>Perhaps most intriguingly, drums are handled by **Fujiwara Kamui**, a character designer and manga artist—a detail that hints at KYMM potentially exploring the theatrical, artistic identity that defines Visual Kei's visual aesthetic as much as its music. This crossover between visual art and music production is quintessentially VK.</p>
<p>While details about their sound remain limited, the roster alone suggests KYMM will sit comfortably within the melodic, guitar-driven side of the Visual Kei spectrum. With Yuh's experience from vistlip—a band known for intricate arrangements and emotional depth—audiences can likely expect something polished yet artistic.</p>
<p>For Western VK fans, this is a band worth watching. The combination of members from the underground circuit, paired with an artist whose visual design background might inform their stage presence, signals genuine creative ambition rather than a casual side project.</p>
<p>Keep an eye on KYMM's official channels for upcoming live dates and music releases. As always, support new VK bands early—you might be watching the next cult favorite take shape.</p>
