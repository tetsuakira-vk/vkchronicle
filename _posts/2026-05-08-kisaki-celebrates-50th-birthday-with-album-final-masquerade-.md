For over three decades, KISAKI has been a godfather figure in Visual Kei—one of the genre's true architects and a constant driving force shaping the scene. Now, as he marks his 50th birthday in 2026, the legendary bassist and bandleader is marking the milestone in style with a memorial album, sold-out celebration shows, and fresh announcements that prove his influence remains undiminished.

In March, KISAKI released *Voice in Sadness*, a star-studded memorial album featuring guest appearances from members of today's most important Visual Kei bands. The production philosophy was clear: honor the past while showcasing the present scene's vitality. The album contains 12 tracks, including three new songs plus remixes and remastered material, packaged in a luxe 24-page photo booklet.

The celebration kicked off with "BEYOND THE KINGDOM -Fest of Excellence-" at Osaka's BigCat on March 15—a show that sold out so completely the venue's doors couldn't fully close. Now, KISAKI is doubling down with a follow-up event and new music on the horizon.

**What's Coming Next:**

**New Album**: A fresh studio release drops September 30, 2026. Details coming soon.

**"BEYOND THE KINGDOM -Final Masquerade-"**: October 24, 2026 at Tokyo's Blaze Gotanda. This is billed as the final chapter of the birthday celebration series, and it's stacked. The KISAKI PROJECT will headline, fronted by vocalist ryo (D'elsquel, 9GOATS BLACK OUT, and others) alongside guest guitarists HIZAKI (Versailles, Jupiter) and Kohei Iyoda. The stellar lineup includes defspiral, THE BEETHOVEN, NEGA, ClearVeil, Phobia, and more—capped by a massive all-artist jam session.

**Tickets**: VIP passes (¥18,000, including premium goods) go on sale May 1–31 via Lorelei Entertainment. General admission (¥7,000 presale / ¥7,300 door) hits e-plus on June 6 at 10 AM JST. Drinks are extra.

For Western fans unable to attend, the September album release is essential. For those lucky enough to be in Tokyo, this is unmissable.
