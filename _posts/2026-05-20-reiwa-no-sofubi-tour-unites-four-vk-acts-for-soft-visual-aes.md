Four Visual Kei acts are joining forces this autumn to celebrate the resurgence of "soft visual" — the lyrically-driven, melody-focused strain of VK that flourished in the late '90s and early 2000s.

The tour, titled "令和のソフビ" (*Reiwa no Sofubi* / "Soft Visual of the Reiwa Era"), launches in October 2026 and emphasizes emotion over aggression, beauty over raw power. It's a deliberate reclamation of an aesthetic that feels both nostalgic and urgently contemporary.

## The Lineup

**heidi.** brings enduring songwriting craft and disciplined live presence — they've built a steady fanbase on introspective melodies and genuine emotional delivery. **HELLBROTH** counters with sharp yet delicate sensibilities, balancing intensity with restraint. **The Benjamin** represents what modern soft visual could be: literary worldbuilding meets distinctly Japanese melodic sensibility. And **LAY ABOUT WORLD** rounds out the bill with kinetic energy and forward-thinking arrangements that refuse to simply pastiche the past.

![Four Visual Kei musicians performing on stage](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fvijuttoke.com%2Fcontents%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2FLAW_240701_ALL-580x381.jpg&w=800&output=jpg)

The aesthetic here isn't pure revivalism. Rather, it's asking: what does "soft" Visual Kei sound like in 2026? The answer appears to be refined, literary, and unapologetically emotional — qualities that feel refreshingly countercultural in today's music landscape.

## Tour Details

Four dates across Japan:

- **October 25** — HEAVEN'S ROCK Utsunomiya (Tochigi)
- **November 2** — Shinsaibashi soma (Osaka)
- **November 3** — Yokkaichi CLUB CHAOS (Mie)
- **November 7** — Takadanobaba CLUB PHASE (Tokyo)

All shows run 16:00 doors / 16:30 start, concluding around 20:05. Admission is ¥5,000 advance / ¥5,500 day-of, plus one drink charge.

## Ticketing

Preorder (lottery) runs August 22–31, 2026 via TICKET PAY. General advance sales begin September 26. Tickets are sold through Famima and 7-Eleven convenience stores.
